Is He Married? Dating And Relationship

While wrestling star Shelton Benjamin’s professional career has always been in the spotlight, his personal life has always remained a mystery. Who is Shelton Benjamin wife? Read the article till the end to find out. 

Shelton Benjamin has not been open about his relationship and personal life. But, various sources have reported that he is still single. 

However, the 48-year-old has two daughters. 

Shelton James Benjamin, born on July 9, 1975, is a celebrated American professional wrestler known for his remarkable career in WWE.

He began his journey in WWE’s developmental territory, Ohio Valley Wrestling (OVW), winning the OVW Southern Tag Team Championship thrice with Brock Lesnar and once with Rodney Mack.

His rise to the main roster in 2002 led to the formation of Team Angle with Kurt Angle and Charlie Haas, and later, The World’s Greatest Tag Team with Haas.

Benjamin’s impressive accolades include the Intercontinental Championship, United States Championship, WWE Tag Team Championship, and even the WWE 24/7 Championship.

Despite not capturing a world title, his athleticism and skill have earned him a place as one of WWE’s legendary competitors, leaving a lasting impact on the wrestling world.

Shelton Benjamin Wife: Is He Married?

According to various sources, Shelton Benjamin is currently single. There is no information online regarding his marriage with anyone. Therefore, it would be safe to assume that Shelton Benjamin has not been married and does not have a wife. 

However, the wrestler was previously involved with a woman named Dana Massengale. He has a daughter named Kyana with Dana. 

Except that, Shelton’s name has not been linked with other women. The wrestler prefers to keep his personal information under wraps. 

Even his social media site provides no insight into his relationships. 

Shelton Benjamin Kids

Shelton has two daughters named Kyana and Ash, and he is very close with them. He often posts about them and proudly celebrates their achievements on his social media. 

Kyana Benjamin is the elder daughter, born to her father, Shelton Benjamin, and her mother, Dana Massengale. She graduated high school in 2017 from Lubbock High School in Texas. 

Later, she attended Willamette University for a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology and International Studies. Kyana is currently a full-time student at the New Mexico State University, where she is pursuing a Master’s degree in Mental Health Counseling. 

Kyana inherited athletic genes from her father. She played volleyball during her high school and college years. In her high school career, she earned Academic All-District and Academic All-State honors and was chosen as Lubbock’s team MVP.

Similarly, Shelton’s younger daughter, Ash, graduated high school in 2022. She is currently 19 years old. Unlike her elder sister, not much is known about Ash. 

Did Shelton Benjamin leave WWE?

Recently, on September 21, 2023, Shelton wrote on Twitter,

”I have been released from my WWE contract. I thank WWE staff, talent, and, of course, the fans for everything. Something ends something new begins. Looking forward to my next chapter.”

However, Benjamin wasn’t the only one that WWE released on September 21. Other wrestlers like Mustafa Ali, Rick Boogs, Aliyah, Tegan Nox, Emma, Riddick Moss, etc., were also released on the same date as him. 

These releases by WWE occurred as part of a series of layoffs that followed the merger of WWE with UFC into TKO Group Holdings. The layoffs appear to be a cost-cutting measure or a restructuring effort by the company.
