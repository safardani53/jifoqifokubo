THE world's most futuristic motorhome has its own full-size garage, massage chairs and interior styled by a yach designer.

The stunning vehicle, manufactured by Hungarian firm Dembell, is like something from a Hollywood movie.

The elevated driving cab features four seats with full massage options for those long haul journeys.

Property guru Enes Yilmazer took viewers on a tour of the home's super yacht style interior.

Enes described the bathroom, complete with a heated toilet seat and retractable robe hanger rail as "like something from a $50million yacht, it's so impressive".

The kitchen features a Miele oven and wine fridge perfect for hosting a dinner party.

The £1.2million motorhome stand out feature is a garage with parking for a Mercedes 280SL Pagoda.

The garage also features a washer and dryer to keep on top of the laundry piles as you travel.

The influencer described the model as a "spaceship" and likened its luxury to an "exquisite Italian yacht".
