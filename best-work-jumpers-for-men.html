12 best work jumpers for men

GOT your work wardrobe sorted for autumn/winter? The cold may be coming but no stress: we've put together the best work jumpers for men to up your style this season.

Every man needs a stylish selection of jumpers to throw on for work and wear on rotation, as the weather drops.

And whether you're shopping on a budget or are looking to add some investment jumpers into your winter wardrobe, there are some key pieces out there that will see you through the season and add some much-needed warmth to your work attire.

From smart and lightweight jumpers you can layer over shirts to thick knits for maximum cosiness, there's a whole array of jumpers available to suit your job's requirements.

Fine knit V-necks and polo necks are perfect for keeping your look smart and polished, while chunky jumpers and sweaters are ideal if your work takes you outdoors.

Warm up your workwear with our pick of this season's best knits for work below.

    Where to buy mens work jumpers

    The great news is that there is an abundance of stylish men's work jumpers on the market. For quick budget buys that you can quickly buy in store, click and collect or purchase online then places like George, Next, and Sainsburys have a huge selection. For more investment pieces that are made with quality pure wool then House of Bruar, Pringle, Marks & Spencer, and Ralph Lauren have some great pieces too.

    What is the best material for a jumper?

    While jumpers with nylon or polyester make them super easy to wash, pure wool jumpers are always the best when it comes to long-lasting quality. A jumper with a mix can also work well, making it easy to wash while still having all the qualities of a pure wool jumper.
