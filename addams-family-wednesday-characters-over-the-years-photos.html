Addams Family and Wednesday Characters Over the Years: Photos Hollywood Life

Throughout the years, The Addams Family shows and movies have changed but surprisingly, the characters have remained the same & you can see the similarities & differences here.

The Addams Family has become a household name throughout the years ever since cartoonist, Charles Addams, created the family for the New Yorker in 1938. After that, in 1964, The Addams Family aired on ABC for two seasons. In 1991, the first movie  — The Addams Family — was created followed by Addams Family Values in 1993. Since then, the latest installment of the family is the show Wednesday, which aired on Netflix in 2022. Over the course of decades, the shows and movies have changed but surprisingly, the characters have remained remarkably the same. From their outfits to their personalities, you can see how the characters have transformed throughout the years.
