Disney's Dole Whip: Where can I buy the ice cream?

DOLE Whip has served as a Disney dessert staple for decades.

Now, fans will no longer have to travel to a Disney theme park to get their hands on the sweet frozen treat.

Where can I buy Disney's Dole Whip?

For years, Disney's Dole Whip remained solely available at Disney World in Orlando, Florida, and at Disneyland in Anaheim, California.

However, as of the spring of 2023, the popular dessert is now available at various grocery stores across the United States.

On March 10, 2023, PEOPLE reported that "packaged Dole Whip will be available at select retailers" beginning in April, according to a representative for Dole Packaged Foods.

The Instagram account @snackolator then shared the news on May 10, 2023.

The post said, in part: "Dole Whip is arriving at grocery stores nationwide right now!"

It continued: "For those who have never had Dole Whip, it is one of the most popular frozen treats from Disney World and has been available at other theme parks and ice cream shops over the last few years.

"But Dole is now bringing it straight to the grocery stores so you can pretend you're relaxing at Disney as you wolf down a Dole Whip at home."

The account then noted that "so far" the tasty treat has been found at Walmart "and other small grocery chains."

What flavors of Disney's Dole Whip are available in-store?

There are three flavors of Disney's Dole Whip hitting the shelves, including pineapple, mango, and strawberry.

As per people: "Not only will the classic pineapple flavor be offered in the frozen food aisle, but Dole Whip will also be offered in two other fruity flavors — strawberry and mango.

"Each package includes four cups of the fruity dessert."

When did Disney first release the Dole Whip?

Disney first released Dole Whip in 1984 in Disney World's Magic Kingdom Park, specifically at Aloha Isle in the Adventureland region.

It was then introduced to Disneyland in 1986.

Dole Whip has "since has become a staple at multiple places" within Disney World and Disneyland," as per PEOPLE.

Since its debut, the beloved snack has become highly popular and widely known, with Delish referring to it as " a cult classic among Disney fans."
