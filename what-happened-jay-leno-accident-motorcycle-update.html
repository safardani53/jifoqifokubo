What happened to Jay Leno?

JAY LENO has kept himself busy since leaving The Tonight Show by taking care of his car collection and performing shows across the country.

However, he was recently hit with a string of bad luck and now fans want to know what happened.

What happened to Jay Leno?

On November 12, 2022, Leno was working on a 1907 white steam car that had a clogged fuel line.

The former Tonight Show host is no stranger to fixing and restoring old cars, so at the time, the clog was no big deal.

However, chaos erupted after the fuel line busted and spilled gasoline all over his face. Moments later, his face caught on fire.

“I said to my friend, I said, ‘Dave [Killackey] I am on fire here,'” Leno recalled during a TODAY Show appearance, via Deadline.

“I grabbed him by the head and pulled his head into my chest,” Killackey added.

“It was horrific," Killackey recalled, "It was a scary thing. It doesn’t take a genius to say, ‘call 911’.” 

Despite having a face full of burns, Leno did not go to the hospital until the next day. He then spent nine days at Grossman Burn Center in West Hills undergoing treatments.

“When he came in, his burns were deeper than I anticipated. Jay is definitely an outlier in terms of how well he’s healed considering the severity of his injuries," Leno's surgeon, Peter Grossman, told PEOPLE.

Since then, Leno has been on the road to recovery, but less than two months later it was revealed that he was involved in another incident that left him with a broken collarbone, ribs, and cracked kneecaps.

The incident occurred on January 17, 2023, while he was driving a vintage motorcycle in Las Vegas, according to the Las Vegas Review-Journal.

“So I turned down a side street and cut through a parking lot, and unbeknownst to me, some guy had a wire strung across the parking lot but with no flag hanging from it,” Leno told the outlet.

“So, you know, I didn’t see it until it was too late. It just clothesline me and, boom, knocked me off the bike.

“You know, after getting burned up, you get that one for free,” he later joked.

“After that, you’re Harrison Ford, crashing airplanes. You just want to keep your head down.”

Despite several broken bones, Leno insisted that he was alright.

“But I’m OK!” he added. “I’m OK, I’m working. I’m working this weekend.”

Is Jay Leno married?

When Leno is not taking care of his car collection, or spending time in the hospital, he can often be found at home with his wife, Mavis Leno.

Mavis is known for her career as a philanthropist and currently serves on the Board of Directors of the Feminist Majority Foundation.

Her work focuses on making women’s rights a non-negotiable element of a post-Taliban Afghanistan, according to her biography.

Mavis' work with the foundation has been recognized worldwide and it was even nominated for the Nobel Peace Prize in 2002.

The pair tied the knot in 1980 and have been living a comfortable life with no children.
