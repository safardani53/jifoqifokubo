Christina El Moussa, Ant Anstead Honeymoon Photos

Christina El Moussa and Ant Anstead in Bora Bora

Following their surprise "winter wonderland" wedding at their home in Newport Beach, Calif. on December 22, the Flip or Flop star and the Wheeler Dealers host jetted off to Bora Bora for a little rest and relaxation during their honeymoon.

Views From Above

Anstead shared a video from their small plane as they landed on the island, which showed off breathtakingly blue water and a series of small islands. "Honeymoon," he wrote on the video.

Boat Day

The couple snapped a quick selfie during their water taxi to their resort, as Anstead sported a necklace of flowers.

A Temporary Home

El Moussa, who is changing her last name to Anstead, was rendered speechless by the island's views from their boat taxi, and covered her mouth with a manicured hand.

Sunset Smooch

While watching the sun go down over the South Pacific, the newlyweds shared a kiss under the palm trees. "Magic hour," El Moussa captioned the sweet photo.

Basking in the Sun

The pair stayed in an overwater bungalow at the Four Seasons Bora Bora, and Ant enjoyed relaxing for a bit in their private pool by the water's edge.

A Celebratory Glass of Champagne

Anstead posted a boomerang of El Moussa pouring a glass of bubbly to kick off their vacation. "My Wife," he wrote on the video along with an arrow pointing to El Moussa in a bikini and the heart eyes emoji.

Jumping In

While sporting a red bikini, El Moussa took a leap off their villa's private dock into the clear blue water.

Floating in Paradise

After landing, El Moussa floated on her back in the ocean for a bit as she laughed and adjusted her sunglasses.

A Weekly Tradition

As the HGTV star walked down the dock of their bungalow wearing a grey dress and flip flops, she smiled at the camera and said "Happy Anniversary"—a phrase the couple revealed they have said to each other every Sunday since they met.

Showing Off Her Bling

El Moussa was all smiles as she posed for a photo with a glass of champagne while showing off her 8.5-carat diamond wedding band.

Beachside Tiki

While enjoying the view of the French Polynesian island, Anstead posted a video to his Instagram Stories that panned around the water's edge showing two glowing tiki torches. "Honeymooning," he captioned the video, followed by a series of emojis including the British and American flags.

Tropical Selfie

The pair snapepd a selfie as they took sips of their fruity drinks during their last day on the island. "Bora Bora you have been a blast!" Anstead writes. "Soaking up a few last minute cocktails! Then onto Tahiti for a sleep over before heading home! Fully relaxed! Fully recharged."
