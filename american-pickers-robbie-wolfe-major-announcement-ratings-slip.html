American Pickers' Robbie Wolfe ready to get 'hands dirty' as he makes major announcement after ratin

AMERICAN Pickers star Robbie Wolfe has alerted fans that the Antique Archaeology team is on the search for unique pieces to add to their collection.

Robbie divulged in a new social media post that he and the American Pickers crew were on the hunt for vintage collector's items on the west coast.

The reality star posted a photo of him and fellow History Channel personalities Danielle Colby, 47, Jersey Jon, 58, and Mike Wolfe, 59, who is also his brother, on his Instagram page.

They posed in Mike's Antique Archaeology shop in Nashville, Tennessee, smiling to varying degrees.

Robbie revealed in his caption that the team was on their way to California and Arizona in search of relics to obtain.

"The pickers are looking to get their hands dirty in some rare, junkie, historical items," he wrote.

"We're looking for private collections (sorry, no stores!), with fascinating stories.

"Let us know if you or someone you know has our next big pick!"

Robbie included the contact information so that those with valuable finds could reach out to the American Pickers in his caption also.

The post seemed like an attempt to get fans excited so that they would tune into this season of the show and deliver it from the stagnant low ratings.

ARRESTED DEVELOPMENT

American Pickers episodes featuring Mike, Robbie, and Danielle returned on July 5. 

Last week, The U.S. Sun exclusively revealed 918,000 viewers watched the premiere.

But for the July 12 episode, over 100,000 tuned out with ratings dropping to 803,000. 

Ratings were up and down last season.

The March 8 episode of American Pickers brought in 844,000 viewers. 

That was up from the week before, as only 711,000 viewers watched the March 1 episode. 

The season 24 premiere kicked off strong with 943,000 fans watching.

FAN BOYCOTT

Fans have vowed to boycott the show after the firing of fan-favorite star Frank, 59.

One commented of the premiere episode: "I'll only watch American Pickers if it's a rerun with Frank. Carry on."

A second said: "The only episodes of American Pickers I watch is WITH FRANK."

A third asked on the show’s Twitter account: "Why did you screw Frank over?"

FRANK'S FIRING

Frank last appeared on American Pickers during a March 2020 episode, as he took time off to recover from back surgery that left him with 185 stitches and two rods in his spine. 

During his time off the show, Frank lost 65 pounds.

He also told The U.S. Sun that he entered rehab for alcohol addiction in Iowa for 77 days.

Frank revealed his feud with co-star Mike during the 2021 interview with The U.S. Sun.

Frank said: “I haven’t talked to Mike in two years. He knew my back was messed up, but he didn’t call me up and ask how I was doing. That’s just how it is.

"The show is tilted towards him 1,000 percent. I can’t even bend that far down to show you how much.
