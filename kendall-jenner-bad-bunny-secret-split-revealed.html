Kendall Jenner and boyfriend Bad Bunny break up as reason for secret split after rock

KENDALL Jenner and Bad Bunny broke up, reports have revealed.

The couple sparked romance rumors earlier this year but were rarely seen out in public together.

"There's no bad blood," a source close to the A-list celebs told The Messenger, "They are even still in contact. Their relationship had been rocky due to their busy schedules."

The insider also revealed that Kendall, 27, "felt the relationship ran its course."

The news outlet claimed that the split occurred a few weeks ago.

WHAT THEY'VE SAID

Kendall and Bad Bunny, 29, - real name Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio - have kept their relationship private throughout the year.

In an interview with Rolling Stone, Bad Bunny remained vague about his love life.

"People know everything about me, so what's left for me to protect? My private life, my personal life," the singer wrote.

"No comment," Kendall said in an interview with The Wall Street Journal, "I'll go out of my way to do things as privately as possible because I just think that that's the healthier way of dealing with relationships anyway."

FANS REACT

Once the news broke, fans shared their mixed opinions on an internet forum dedicated to the Kardashians.

MORE ABOUT THEIR RELATIONSHIP

On March 8, 2023, the two were photographed sharing affection during a dinner date at Sushi Park in West Hollywood.

Previously, the pair stirred dating speculation when they were seen making out at a Los Angeles club by onlookers in February.

The two fueled romance rumors even further when just days later, they were caught making separate exits from The Bird Streets Club.

That night, it's believed Kendall and Bad Bunny were on a double date with Justin and Hailey Bieber.

It was also previously reported that Kendall and Bad Bunny went on a number of double dates with the pair and were even photographed together at Coachella.

As the months went on, they were spotted at fashion events, dates, concerts, basketball games, and even did a Gucci campaign together.

BIRTHDAY BABE

For weeks, fans have been speculating they split after finding several "clues" in the supermodel's social media posts.

Last month, Kendall enjoyed a birthday trip with friends without her man by her side.

Several other attendees were joined by their partners, however.

The Kardashians star's pal Lauren Perez posted pics of Kendall and other stars gathered at a vineyard for Hailey Bieber's birthday.

The post was captioned: "The best ships are friendships," and featured Kendall, Hailey, and Justin.

Kendall was wearing a black crop top and skirt that showed off her toned figure.

She completed the look with a black-and-white headscarf.

'WHERE IS HE?'

Bad Bunny was noticeably absent from the photos, which left some speculating that their romance is over.

"WHERE IS BAD BUNNY," one fan wondered in the comments.

The sentiment was echoed several times by other concerned fans.

Another person wrote: "No Bad Bunny no like."

Prior to that post, Kendall shared another social media snap that got fans buzzing about a breakup.

She randomly included a new photo to her Instagram feed that appeared to be either a sunset or sunrise.

There was a silhouette of trees and mountains in the frame.

Her caption read: "What's meant for me, will simply find me."

Several fans flocked to her aid in the comments, accusing Bad Bunny of hurting her.

One commenter asked: "Did conejo malo hurt you??!" referencing Bad Bunny.
