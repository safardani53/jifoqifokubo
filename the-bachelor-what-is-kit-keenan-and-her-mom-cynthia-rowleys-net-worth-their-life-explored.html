The Bachelor: What is Kit Keenan and her mom Cynthia Rowleys net worth? Their life explored

‘The Bachelor’ nation got to know a lot about Kit Keenan as she shed light on her luxurious lifestyle. But who is Kit and her mother Cynthia Rowley? Here’s a look at their life and net worth.

The latest episode of the show gave fans a lot to remember. From Victoria Larson leaving the show to Kit talking about her life, there was never a dull moment. This made fans curious about Kit and her mother. To get your mind to ease, we have got all the information right here!

Who is Cynthia Rowley?

Cynthia is an American fashion designer. Her official website describes her brand as “pretty-meets-sporty assortment features ready-to-wear, surf and swim, fitness, accessories, and home decor as well as exclusive collaborations with like-minded brands.”

Her brand focuses on women’s empowerment and “exudes a spontaneous spirit and actively redefines the boundaries of fashion, inspiring women everywhere to take risks and live life fearlessly,” as per the website.

What is Cynthia Rowley’s net worth?

The fashion designer has an estimated net worth of $100 million dollars. Cynthia gave birth to her own fashion line in the 80s. Since then, her brand has grown and she has managed to make a name for herself in the fashion industry.

She has a wide variety of things at her store. From shoes, accessories, clothes to cosmetics, perfumes, and others, Cynthia’s brand offers a lot. At the same time, she has stores located in various countries.

Who is Kit Keenan?

Kit is the oldest daughter of Cynthia. Her Instagram bio describes her as a “student, designer, small business owner.” Kit is very close to Cynthia and the pair even host a podcast together. Their podcast ‘Ageless‘ showcases “thoughtful discussions with female leaders and surprise guests.”

Just like Cynthia, Kit also has a fashion line of her own which she started in 2018. Kit opened up about the type of man she wants as she told ABC she “is looking for a man who is genuine, open and honest.”

What is Kit Keenan’s net worth?

As per Gossip Gist, Kit has a net worth between $100k-$500k. Her appearance on the show is definitely going to boost her popularity. At the same time, fans will get to know a lot more about Kit as she continues to appear on the show.

She has already given people an idea of her lavish lifestyle while talking about her luxurious cars and Louboutin shoes. We will just have to wait and see what more does she have in store for us.
