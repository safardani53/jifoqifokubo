What to know about NLE Choppa's ex-girlfriend

MEMPHIS-born rapper NLE Choppa dated Marissa Da'Nae from 2021 to 2022.

Many fans are curious about who the influencer is and why the couple split.

Who is NLE Choppa's ex-girlfriend?

NLE Choppa, born on November 1, 2002, in Memphis, Tennessee, grew to fame for his 2019 hit Shotta Flow.

Two years later, he began dating Marissa Da'Nae, a lifestyle vlogger on YouTube and an Instagram influencer.

Da'Nae has 887k followers on her Instagram and 31.4k subscribers on her YouTube.

She mostly posts lifestyle content and vlogs on YouTube, while her Instagram is mostly about her style and fashion collaborations.

She is also the owner of a boutique called Brazy But Lavish Boutique and a hair salon called Lavish Hair.

Why did NLE Choppa and Marissa Da'Nae break up?

When the two broke up in September 2022, NLE Choppa released a statement about their breakup, while Da'Nae went live on Instagram, shedding tears over her ex-lover.

NLE's Twitter statement said: "I Am Single, Im Man Enough To Admit I’m Not Ready, And I Have Some Growing To Do."

NLE Choppa responded to his ex's breakdown on Instagram Live with a YouTube video further explaining their situation.

In it, he shared that he wanted to live a polygamous lifestyle but that despite Da'Nae agreeing to it, she wasn't happy with it.

He said: “Marissa was willing to give up all of these things that she felt, that she was going to get out of life.

"She was with me in a sense of hope out of life, and I grew out of it. She was willing to do everything… her mind and her heart wanted to do it, but her soul… that was not what her soul wanted.”

Is Marissa Da'Nae NLE Choppa's baby mama?

NLE Choppa has one daughter named Clover, but she was born in 2020 to a woman named Mariah.

In February 2022, Marissa Da'Nae announced that she had miscarried a son that belonged to NLE Choppa.

The couple announced they were having a baby boy and that his name would be Seven Da’Shun Potts, taking the rapper's real last name, in late 2021.

In an Instagram post about their miscarriage, the influencer wrote a sweet post.

It read: “Talking bout this is so hard, But with Bryson [Potts] next to me and being my supporter, this has helped me more.

"Losing our Son is so hard for me, I couldn’t function. But he has pulled me through and I’m thankful to have you by my side and with me along this journey.”
