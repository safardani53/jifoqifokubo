Brad Pitt &amp; Angelina Jolie In Then And Now Photos Hollywood Life

Mr. and Mrs. Smith was more than just a killer 2005 romance-action film. It was the project where Brad Pitt, 57, and Angelina Jolie, 45, fell madly in love. The chemistry between the two actors was so intense that the film was a smash hit at the box office. But it also drew tons of curiosity, as by the time it hit theaters on June 7, 2005, Brad and wife Jennifer Aniston were divorcing, and photos of Brangie as a couple on vacation in Kenya had emerged in Mar. 2005, confirming rumors of passionate feelings on set that had carried over into real life.

Now Mr. and Mrs. Smith is coming back as a series in 2022 on Amazon starring Atlanta‘s Donald Glover and Fleabag‘s Phoebe Waller-Bridge in the title roles. While those two are going to crush it as a married assassin vs. assassin couple, Brad and Angelina have already had plenty of style moments in real life that mirrored their film roles. Unlike the movie, the pair’s hot romance turned into a family life with six kids and a 2014 marriage, before Angelina sadly filed for divorce in 2016. Here’s some of their stylish moments from Mr. and Mrs. Smith with how Brad and Angie later wore the looks in real life.

Angelina’s strapless dominatrix top

Angelina rocked a black dominatrix top in the 2005 film, while mirroring the look in a 2019 evening gown. Photo credit: Everett Collection/AP

Early on in the film, assassin Angelina is seen as a seductress, wearing a tight black leather dominatrix top before killing off one of her targets. She paired it with a matching short skirt, thigh-high stockings with garters and fishnet arm sleeves. Angelina still loves a sleeveless glam black top, as she rocked a strapless shiny black evening gown at the premiere of Maleficent: Mistress Of Evil in Rome, Italy on Oct. 7, 2019.

Angelina’s stylish black trench coat

Whether on screen in 2005 or shopping in L.A. in 2021, Angelina knows how to rock a black trench coat. Photo credit: Everett Collection/BACKGRID.

Nearly 16 years later, Angelina still looks like chic perfection in a black trench coat. In Mr. and Mrs. Smith, she wore the belted black trench to hide her dominatrix outfit before going in for her kill. She put it back on to jump out of a window to make her dramatic escape from the tall building, using an umbrella to help slow down her descent. In real life, Angelina is still just as stunning in the look, as she donned a black trench to head out shopping with daughters Zahara, 16, and Shiloh, 14, on Feb. 6, 2021.

Brad and Angelina’s sexy black suits and dresses

In the 2005 film, Brad and Angelina’s husband and wife pair John and Jane Smith had a passionate dance while discussing how they had been hired to kill each other. They both looked so stylish, with Brad in a black suit and Angie in a plunging sleeveless gown with a high thigh slit. The pair proved they looked just as good in black formalwear on red carpets during their years as a real-life couple. Angelina famously wore a black velvet Atlelier Versace gown to the 2012 Oscars that featured a thigh high slit up the front, which she made the most of showing her sexy leg.

Brad can still rock a tie-free suit like no one else

Brad Pitt is so darn stylish both on and off screen when he wears suit jackets. Photo credit: Everett Collection/AP

Brad Pitt looks so good in a suit, and totally owns the more casual side when he goes without a tie. He wore a tan jacket along with a white pinstripe shirt underneath in a scene from Mr. and Mrs. Smith. He still looked so dapper 15 years later, as Brad donned a black herringbone jacket over a black shirt to attend the Maltin Modern Masters Awards at the 35th Santa Barbara International Film Festival on Jan. 22, 2020. His hair was grown out from the cropped cut he had in the film, but Brad’s style remained perfection. He looks so good in a suit that Brad was named Italian menswear couture house Brioni‘s face of their Fall/Winter 2020 “Tailoring Legends” campaign.

Angelina proves a white shirt never goes out of style

Theres nothing more classic that a white button up shirt, and Angie looks great in the look both on screen and off. Photo credit: Everett Collection/AP

Angelina wore just a white man’s dress shirt as she and Brad’s characters spent a night in their marital home trying to kill each other with every kind of high powered weapon they could grab (and they each had huge stashes). They eventually blew up their house! In real life, Angelina still is so stylish in just a simple white blouse, as she’s seen here speaking at CinemaCon in Las Vegas on March 25, 2014.
