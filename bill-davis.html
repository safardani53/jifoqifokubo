Who is Bill Davis:

Bill Davis is a famous Politician. He was born on July 30, 1929 and his birthplace is Canada. Bill is also well known as, Member of the Ontario PC Party who served as the 18th Premier of Ontario from 1971 to 1985. He also served his country as its Minister of Education during the mid 1960’s and he notably helped create both Trent University and Brock University as well as another twenty-two community colleges.

Bill is originated from Canada. He served as a Canadian premier at the same time as Premier of Alberta Peter Lougheed.
