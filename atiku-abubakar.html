Who is Atiku Abubakar:

Atiku Abubakar is a famous Politician. He was born on November 25, 1946 and his birthplace is Nigeria. Atiku is also well known as, Nigerian politician who served as Vice President of Nigeria between 1999-2007 as a member of the People’s Democratic Party (PDP). He won the presidential nomination of the PDP in October 2018 en route to his fourth career presidential bid in the general elections. 

Atiku is originated from Nigeria. He served as a Vice President for Olusegun Obasanjo during his term between 1999-2007.
