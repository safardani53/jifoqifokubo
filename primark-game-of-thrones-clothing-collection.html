Primark is selling a limited-edition Game of Thrones clothing range priced from just 7

PRIMARK is selling a range of Game of Thrones clothing so you can watch the finale in style.

With fans eagerly waiting for the last ever season, the high-street store has got you covered for your bingeing sessions on the sofa.

Expect crop tops, T-shirts, comfy leggings and even night shirts for you to cosy up in front of the telly in.

Announcing the collection on their Instagram page, Primark posted: “The final season is coming…

“Make sure you get your hands on our exclusive #GameOfThrones collection to mark the occasion!”

A grey cropped top the model sports costs £10, and the rest of the range is also a bargain with some pieces as little as £7.

GOT fans can’t wait to snap up the new wardrobe, with one person commenting online: “I want one.”

Another said: “Shall we for the release?”

A third simply wrote “love” on the post, which has racked up nearly 5,000 likes.

But as Primark doesn’t sell online, it’s time to hit the high street to get your hands on the range.
