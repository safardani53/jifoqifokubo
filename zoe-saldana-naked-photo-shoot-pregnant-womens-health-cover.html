Zoe’s Ballerina Past Keeps Her In Shape

For those of you that didn’t know, Zoe started taking ballet classes when she was 10 years old.

Even though she doesn’t dance often anymore, her body has kept a lot of the sleek toning years of training gave her. However, she’s handling the changes pretty well.

“My body is less toned,” she tells the magazine. “I do look in the mirror and see things I don’t want. My first reaction is [a gasp] and then I breathe and I think, ‘I’m a woman, I’m 36, my body is changing.’”
