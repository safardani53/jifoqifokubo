Omarion Biography

In 2003, Omarion was seen dating Jennifer Freeman as his girlfriend. Jennifer Freeman is an actress from the United States and she is famous for her role in the TV series My Wife and Kids. Together they also appeared in the movie You Got Served. They broke up in 2005.

Omarion remained a single life for several years and then he was seen having romantic love affairs with Apryl Jones. As a loving couple, they have also been parents once. He has also expressed his desire to get married to her and made her his loving wife soon. He is not married and divorced yet.

While Omarion was in high school, he was active in the high school football team. There he was also famous for his quirky dance moves. It is said that he appeared in the local and school shows since he was five. He was also active in several commercials for Kelloggs Corn Pops and McDonald's. He made his musical debut as the lead singer of a boy band B2K. Their debut album B2K was released on the 12th of March 2002. Their second album Pandemonium! was released at the end of 2002. Later on, he began pursuing his solo career and his debut solo album O was released on the 22nd of February 2005. His second album 21 was released on the 26th of December 2006. He has also been active in acting since 2004. He made his acting debut by appearing in the movie You Got Served. His net worth is said to be around $8 million.
