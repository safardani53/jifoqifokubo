Vanderpump Rules' Ariana Madix goes nearly naked in red 'revenge' reunion dress for new pic- and fan

VANDERPUMP Rules star Ariana Madix has left fans buzzing after she went nearly naked in a red dress for the reality TV show's reunion.

Bravo shared a sneak peek of what the show's stars will be wearing on reunion night including Ariana, 37, and co-star Lala Kent, 32.

Lala, 32, stunned in a metallic cheetah print dress that showed off her toned leg along with a little peek of her stomach.

However, all eyes were on Ariana, 37, as fans described her as wearing a "revenge dress" amid the cheating scandal between ex Tom Sandoval and co-star Raquel Leviss.

The Vanderpump Rules star wore many silver rings as well as a long-sleeved red dress that left little to the imagination.

Fans showed love for Ariana in the comment section as one person exclaimed: "Quite the red revenge dress for Ariana."

SEATING SQUABBLE

Bravo recently released the official seating charts for the reunion, revealing to fans that they settled on two different arrangements to allow for Raquel, 26, and VPR co-star Scheana Shay to appear in person without any issue.

Most read in Entertainment

Raquel filed for and was awarded a temporary restraining order against Scheana, claiming her former gal pal hit her during a physical altercation in New York City after filming an episode of Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen.

The alleged incident is said to have happened after Raquel confessed to being romantically involved with Tom, just before the news went public.

TMZ was the first outlet to report on the affair and Tom and Ariana's subsequent split.

Host Andy will sit in the center, as is tradition on all Bravo reunion shows.

He will have Ariana to one side of him and Lisa Vanderpump, 69 – who owns the restaurants at the center of VPR – on the other.

Seated next to Lisa on Andy's left side will be Tom, followed by Raquel and then Tom Schwartz.

On his right, sitting next to Ariana, will be Katie Maloney followed by James Kennedy and Lala.

When Scheana is on stage, Raquel will not be.

Instead, the two Toms – who own TomTom and Schwartz & Sandy's together – will move next to one another and Scheana will be seated at the end.

Prior to Bravo's big reveal, many fans had wondered how Bravo would handle filming the reunion with Raquel and Scheana while respecting the constraints of the restraining order.

While the order is in place, the VPR co-stars cannot come within 100 feet of one another.

'TOTAL DEVASTATION'

News of the breakup between Tom and Ariana left fans stunned.

The U.S. Sun spoke exclusively to a source close to the TV stars in the wake of their split news.

According to the insider, Ariana did not see the split – or the alleged affair – coming.

"Ariana was completely blindsided by the cheating news this week. She had no idea. Completely no idea," the source said.

"No one in the friend group knew. Raquel has been hanging out with Tom [Schwartz] more but everyone just thought she was hooking up with [Tom.]"

The insider went on to say: "No one suspected she was with Sandoval. Ariana found out from a friend during Tom's show on Wednesday night while Raquel was in New York.

"She immediately confronted Tom and made it clear she wouldn't tolerate that and made the decision they were done almost immediately."
