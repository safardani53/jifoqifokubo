Cadbury launch Dream ice cream stick just in time for the summer and it looks amazing

CALLING all white chocolate fans, your prayers have been answered.

Magnum's got some serious competition, as Cadbury has brought back its popular Dream chocolate bar - in ice cream form.

Cadbury broke hearts across the nation when it axed the popular Dream bar, which used to feature heavily on ad breaks during Corrie.

It's still produced in Australia, New Zealand and South Africa, but that's not much good to us as you can't get it here in Britain.

But that's not the case with the Dream Stick ice cream, which has just been launched in the UK.

According to the Cadbury website, it's a "vanilla flavour ice cream covered in Cadbury Dream white chocolate". Sounds good to us.
