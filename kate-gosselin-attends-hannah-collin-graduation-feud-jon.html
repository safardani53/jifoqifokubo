Kate Gosselin surprises ex Jon by showing up to estranged kids' Hannah and Collin's high school grad

HANNAH and Collin Gosselin have graduated from high school in front of their proud dad Jon and a surprise guest - their estranged mom Kate.

Jon, 46, who has had full custody of both Hannah and Collin, was surprised ex Kate, 48, decided to come to their high school ceremony, as she has been absent for a lot of their lives after the former couple went through a long and bitter custody battle, a source told The U.S. Sun.

Hannah and Collin, both 19, donned red gowns as they posed for pictures with their dad at their Pennsylvania high school.

Jon and his two kids arrived at the ceremony in Collin's new convertible Mustang.

Jon's mom and his ex-girlfriend Colleen also attended the ceremony and sat behind Kate.

A source said Jon knew that Kate would be attending because she has started talking to Hannah again, but was still surprised at her decision to come.

The exes did not speak to each other throughout the event.

Afterward, the dad of six cooked a barbecue for friends and family to celebrate.

A source told The U.S. Sun: “Jon was super proud to see his kids graduate. They’ve all been through a lot these past few years so it was a really nice family celebration.

RECONNECTION

Jon previously revealed how Hannah reconnected with her mom and the siblings in her care Alexis, Leah, Joel and Aaden on the sextuplets' 19th birthdays.

'I'LL NEVER GIVE UP'

But he says the door is always open when it comes to having a relationship with the four sextuplets, and twins Mady and Cara, 22.

“The door is always open to rekindle any kind of relationship. I've tried for years for any interaction with any of my other kids, but nothing yet.”

The former Jon & Kate Plus 8 stars were involved in a nasty custody battle through the years that ended when the children turned 18.
