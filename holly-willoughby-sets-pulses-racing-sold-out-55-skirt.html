Holly Willoughby sets pulses racing in 55 mini skirt - and its sold out already

HOLLY Willoughby’s daring mini skirt has totally sold out – mere hours after she wore it on Thursday’s episode of This Morning.

The 39-year-old TV presenter’s black, buckle-belted skirt from fashion chain And Other Stories, cost a reasonable £55 before it became unavailable to buy.

Holly teamed her uber-popular skirt with the lace-trim Briona shirt from Ted Baker, which cost £119 – but that’s now completely sold out, too.

As always, the mum-of-three shared her TV look on her Instagram page, where seven million followers wait to see what she’ll wear next.

In her caption, she wrote: Morning Thursday... deep breath and off we go... see you on @thismorning at 10am...

"Sharing our must watch tv ideas at the top of the show... all suggestions welcomed! #hwstyle💁🏼 skirt by @andotherstories shirt by @ted_baker ❤️"

It’s certainly not the first time she’s helped to sell out a fashion item, either.

Style star Holly’s influence helped sell out an outfit worth over £1,000 – a £395 dress AND a pair of £795 Manolo Blahnik shoes – which she wore on This Morning's panto episode.

And fans went wild for her 2018 Marks & Spencer collection – quickly selling out most of the range.

Though you’ve still time to imitate some of Holly’s wardrobe if you’ve got the budget to match.
