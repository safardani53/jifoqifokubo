Hunky castle jouster bombarded with saucy messages from lusty ladies

LUSTY ladies are getting in a lather over a castle’s long-haired lancer.

Thousands think that hunky Will Arnold is joust the job and fancy getting rampant on the ramparts with him.

They have been bombarding him with saucy messages, while video clips of Will in action have been viewed one million times.

Will, 32, is the star attraction in the Wars of the Roses jousting re-enactments at Warwick Castle.

The castle is a popular Sun Superdays destination for families.

And mums are particularly keen for a knight of passion with Will.
