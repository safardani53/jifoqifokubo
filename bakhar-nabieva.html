Bakhar Nabieva Biography

Bakhar Nabieva is a female bodybuilder and Internet personality best known for her prolific Instagram account on which she posts footages of herself working out. Bakhar, who ventured into fitness training to defend her classmates who bullied her skinny physique, eventually has become one of the most sought fitness models of the generation.

Early Life, Education

Bakhar Nabieva was born on , in Baku, Azerbaijan. Her birth sign is Aries. She was raised by her parents in Baku where she attended a school.

She was a skinny girl at school because of which she used to be bullied and the bullying led her to become a bodybuilder. She is now an inspiration for thousands.

Career & Achievements

Bakhar Nabieva is famous for her muscular physique, especially her legs. She became popular on Instagram for her fitness-related photos and video clips of her working out.

She uploaded her first post on Instagram on March 29, 2015, and has successfully gathered over 2.5 million followers on her Instagram.

She and Jessica Arevalo, a lifetime natural athlete with more than 2.5 million followers on her Instagram, both are known for their fitness-centric Instagram accounts.

Nabieva is also known as Miss Iron Bum. Being a fitness trainer, she also owns a gym where she trains many who wants to get a body like her.

Personal Life

Bakhar Nabieva is pretty secretive about her personal life. It's unknown whether she is single, dating or already married.

However, in 2017, she shared an Instagram photo in which she appeared holding hands with a man with two children, a boy, and a girl, around them.

She hasn't disclosed if the guy was her husband or boyfriend, or if the kids belong to her.

Net Worth & Endorsements

After getting fame as a female bodybuilder, Nabieva received many endorsements deals, including a deal with a big business company, Dragon Pharma Gorilla Wear USA. She is recognized by many other top fitness brands as well.

She earns a lot of money from her modeling and fitness training career which adds up to her estimated net worth of $250 thousand.

She also has a self-titled YouTube channel with more than 17K subscribers and according to Social Blade, she gets $7 to $112 (per month) through it.
