The Vamps tickets on sale now - how to go to 10th anniversary tour

THE Vamps have announced a huge 2022 Greatest hits tours, and it's time to get your tickets.

To mark 10 years together they're going on tour and here's how you can be part of it.

The Vamps have announced their Greatest Hits Arena tour that will take place across the UK and Ireland.

Available from ticketmaster.co.uk, on July 1 at 10am, make sure you set those alarms now.

Eager fans can also access a pre-sale by signing up on their website or by bordering the Vamps' official zine.

Just don't hang about, as these are sure to sell out fast.

The boy band announced the tour this week, posting on Twitter that their tour will hit the road later this year.

The band tweeted: “Our Greatest Hits Arena tour kicks off in the UK & Ireland this November, we cannot wait to see you all and to celebrate the last 10 years together.”

Also, posting an adorable montage of the group over the last 10 years.

The news has been well received by fans, with many commenting excitedly under the post.

Want to see them live? Don't miss out, tickets go on sale July 1 at 10am from Ticketmaster here.

If you really can't wait, make sure to sign up to the pre-sale on their website or by pre-ordering  the Vamps’ official zine from their website.

It costs £24.99 and includes a 68 page zine book, a CD compiled by the Vamps, including five unreleased tracks, postcards, art cards and much more extra content.
