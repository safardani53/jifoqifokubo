Kourtney Kardashian and Chris Reda: Dating?!

Sorry, Scott. It looks like in spite of your best efforts to get back together with Kourtney Kardashian, the mother of your three kids is ready to move on with a new dude.

TMZ is reporting that over the weekend, Kourtney was spotted on what appeared to be a date with a man named Chris Reda.

Reda is a longtime friend of both Scott and Kourtney’s, and he’s even partied with Kard clan on Keeping Up With the Kardashians.

Insiders say Reda recently visited Disick in rehab, so it’s possible he was simply giving Kourtney an update on her baby daddy’s progress.

But if that’s the case, it’s a bit odd that Kourtney brought her kids on the rendezvous (to say nothing of the fact that she joined Reda for dinner on a Saturday evening just to get the low-down on her ex).

Kourtney’s been more private than ever in the months since she dumped Disick, so it may be a long time before we find out if she and Reda are more than just friends.

Of course, that won’t keep the rumors from flying. Fans are already speculating that Reda is the reason Scott called Kourtney from rehab in a desperate effort to win her back last week.
