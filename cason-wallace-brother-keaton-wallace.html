Cason Wallace Brother Keaton Wallace Is Playing In NBA G League

The 2023 NBA Draft prospect Cason Wallace grew up with a brother named Keaton Wallace, who currently plays in the NBA G League.

Cason’s brother, Keaton, was undrafted in the 2021 NBA Draft and has not yet played in the NBA.

The younger sibling, Cason, considers his brother the most significant influence in his life and hopes to fulfill the dream of the Wallace family by playing on the grand stage.

Cason Wallace, born on November 7, 2003, is a college basketball player playing for the Kentucky Wildcats of the Southeastern Conference (SEC).

The player plays as a point guard and shooting guard for the Wildcats men’s basketball team.

In his freshman year, Wallace played 32 games and averaged 11.7 points per game.

In addition, he featured in prestigious games like McDonald’s All-American and Jordan Brand Classic in 2022.

Cason Wallace Brother Keaton Plays In The NBA G League

Cason Wallace was born on November 7, 2003, to parents Kimberly and Mike Wallace.

He grew up with an elder brother named Keaton Wallace (born: February 26, 1999). The age gap between the Wallace brothers is of four years.

The Wallace brothers’ parents had enrolled both sons in basketball training from a young age.

The older brother is a professional basketball player who currently plays for the Ontario Clippers of the NBA G League.

On October 23, 2021, the Wisconsin Herd selected Keaton in the second round of the 2021 NBA G League draft.

Eventually, the Wisconsin Herd traded him to the Agua Caliente Clippers on the same day.

On February 21, 2023, he agreed to a two-way deal with the Los Angeles and Ontario Clippers.

However, the Clippers released the player on March 1, and they again resigned him three days later, on March 4, 2023.

Before playing professionally, he played basketball for Richardson High School and collegiate basketball at UTSA.

During his college years, he was named to the second-team All-Conference USA three times and the 2018 Conference USA All-Freshman Team.

Girlfriend: Tasharian Robinson

Regarding Keaton’s personal details, he is in a relationship with Tasharian Robinson. The couple are expecting a baby this year.

The pair probably met when they were both students at UTSA. Tasharin attended UTSA for her freshman year but later transferred to Stephen F. Austin State University.

In addition, she also played collegiate basketball like her better half.

Tasharian currently owns Chef Tee, which provides meal prep services for student-athletes on campus as well as people in the community. 

Cason Wallace’s Injury

On March 1, 2023, Kentucky lost against the Commodores, the game where Wallace suffered from a lower left leg injury.

Due to his injury during the game, the guard had to leave the game in the second half of the game, and the player later didn’t return for the contest.

Additionally, when the injury happened, the Cats were in offensive possession with about 18:20 left in the second half.

Moreover, he made an effort to stand up and put weight on the left side of his body after the injury but sat down right away.

“Cason, they X-rayed. He’s fine. It’s not swelled,”. “I’m hoping he will be OK for Saturday (at Arkansas), but I don’t know.” Wildcats head coach John Calipari stated.

Wallace also missed the victory at Ole Miss due to an injury. However, Cason’s injury was not the only highlight of that night. 

In the first half of the game, Liam Robbins, a member of Vanderbilt, also sustained an injury to his left leg. Like the point guard, he also didn’t return later in the game.
