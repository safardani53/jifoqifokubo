6 Best Gift Experiences For Her | The Sun UK

NOTHING'S more refreshing than a day spent doing something fun and different.

So if you’re looking for an out-of-the-ordinary present to surprise your mum, sister or best mate, our round-up of the best gift experiences for her is bound to inspire you.

An experience voucher is a great way to give your loved one the opportunity to try something they’ve never tried before, in an easy, low-commitment and totally enjoyable way.

If you’ve not seen much of each other recently, buying her a voucher for two is also a great way to commit to spending some quality time with each other, whether that means a night away or simply a couple of hours enjoyed at a restaurant or doing an activity together.

Read on for our picks of the best experience days for her.

Note: Due to the current pandemic situation, vendors’ courses and experiences can be suspended or postponed at short notice – do check with your supplier for their individual recommendations when booking.

This article and featured products have been independently chosen by Sun journalists. It contains links which are ads, and if you click a link and buy a product we will earn revenue.

1. Buyagift Store

  • (AD) One-night hotel hideaways experience box, £79.99 from Amazon – buy here

    • Give your partner, mum or mate the gift of a quick escape with this one-night hotel break experience voucher for two.

    You’ll also be giving her the gift of choice as there are over 430 properties to choose from, including beautiful hotels by the coast, quirky countryside guest houses and offbeat glamping experiences – and they all include breakfast.

    Fingers crossed she decides to take you with her!

    2. Activity Superstore

  • (AD) MAC makeover and photoshoot for two, £48.99 from Amazon – buy here

    • Make her feel like a film star for the day with this fantastic pampering experience she can enjoy with her mum or her best mate.

    After a glass of bubbly on arrival, they’ll have their nails done before a qualified make-up artist transforms their look with the full range of MAC products and strip eyelash extensions.

    Next, their hair will be styled and finally, a team of professional female photographers will capture some stunning new photos of them looking glam and feeling amazing, with their favourite shot ready to take away with them at the end of this most memorable of days.

    The experience is available at Middlesborough, Manchester and Leeds and lasts for three to four hours.

    3. Virgin Experience Days

    • Macaron making class, £90 from Virgin Experience Days – buy here

    If she’s a fan of the sweet French fancies, why not treat her to a fun macaron making class held at the highly-regarded Good Housekeeping Institute Cookery School in central London?

    There’s a lot to learn too: the three-hour course promises to share the secrets of Italian meringue, mastering the art of the perfectly smooth shell and even how to get those much-desired “ruffled feet’’ (you’ll have to ask her afterwards).

    It’s a fantastic afternoon experience for budding and experienced bakers alike.

    4. Brigit’s Bakery

    • Vintage afternoon tea bus tour, £110 for two from Brigit’s Bakery – buy here

    Indulge in a traditional afternoon tea complete with scones, jam and clotted cream as you trundle past some of London’s most famous sights and attractions on a vintage London Routemaster bus.

    The onboard afternoon tea includes a delightful selection of French and English teatime treats including a delectable array of sandwiches, cakes and pastries – plus champagne, wine and seasonal cocktails are available too (at an additional charge).

    It’s a great way to wind up a day’s shopping in the capital and would make a wonderful gift for someone special.

    5. Red Letter Days

    • Pottery experience and afternoon tea at The World of Wedgwood, £100 for two from Red Letter Days – buy here

    This fascinating afternoon starts with a self-guided tour of the Wedgwood factory and museum to learn all about the history and production of the famous British china.

    After the tour, she can try her hand at throwing a pot in the Master Craft Studio and will also visit the Decorating Studio, where she'll decorate a plate with her own personal design as a memento of the day.

    The outing is completed by a beautiful afternoon tea (served on Wedgwood china, of course) – a fun and creative day out for pottery-lovers.

    6. The Secret Scent Société

    • One-day perfume making workshop, £99 from The Secret Scent Société – buy here

    She’ll discover the magical world of the perfumer and create her own personalised fragrance at this delightful workshop in London’s Hatton Garden.

    As well as a 12ml bottle of her own distinctive perfume, she’ll also go home with a complimentary notepad, fragrance chart and fragrance profiles of her favourite scents.

    If you upgrade to the premium experience, her bespoke scent can even be made into a 50ml perfume, reed diffuser or scented candle.

    A delightfully fragrant way to spend an afternoon...
