Josh Murray- All About The Winner Of The Bachelorette Season 10

Josh Murray, an American television personality, has had two failed engagements. The native of Syracuse, New York rose to prominence after winning the 10th season of ABC’s dating and love reality show The Bachelor. He is the creator and teacher of Murray Fitness, which has facilities in Tampa, Atlanta, and Los Angeles. Here are some wiki facts about Josh, including his bio, net worth, brother, mother, dating life, and engagement to Amanda Stanton.

Josh Murray’s Bio: Parents, Brother, Height

Joshua Allan Murray was born on August 12, 1984, to Dennis and Lauren Murray. Josh Murray is the oldest of three siblings, including a brother, Aaron Murray, and a sister, Stephanie Murray. Josh, who stands at a towering 6 feet 1 inch (1.85 meters), was a basketball player when he was younger. The Milwaukee Brewers selected him in the second round of the 2002 MLB June Amateur Draft. According to wiki sources, one of his brothers, Aaron, is a former Kansas City Chiefs quarterback. Josh has a strong relationship with his parents and brothers, as seen by his social media posts. For example, on Mother’s Day in May 2018, he wished his mother, Lauren, as a role model.

Josh Won The Bachelorette Series At The Age Of 34

Josh debuted as a contestant on The Bachelorette’s 10th season in 2014. Andi Dorfman, 31, is a former assistant district attorney from Atlanta, Georgia. The season finished in July 2014, with Josh Murray as the winner, and he proposed to Andi on August 1, 2014. However, their romance did not survive long, and they divorced in January 2015, less than a year after their engagement.

Following that, Josh participated in other spin-offs of The Bachelors series. In 2016, he was featured in the third season of Bachelors in Paradise. Similarly, he finished second in the series’ second version, The Challenge: Champs vs. Stars, in 2018. Aside from reality shows, he continues to work as an Advisor for the multi-level marketing business Advocare.

Josh called off his engagement to Amanda Stanton

Josh began dating Amanda Stanton when they were both contestants on Bachelor in Paradise. In August 2016, they confirmed their relationship and relocated from Georgia to California to live with Amanda’s two kids, Kinsley and Charlie. The pair announced their engagement during the season finale of Bachelor in Paradise in September 2016. However, they called off their engagement and broke their romance in early January 2017.

Josh Murray’s Relationship Status in 2018

Josh and Amanda were caught kissing and spending time together in Los Angeles in February 2017, only one month after they had been separated. The future, however, had different plans for them. Josh allegedly phoned the police in late March of the same year to assist collect an Audi he had purchased for Amanda. Amanda said that she had agreed to give up the automobile and that the police presence was unnecessary, but Josh Murray’s assistant, Hayley Watts, seemed to disagree.

With that stated, it was revealed that Josh and Amanda are no longer in a romantic relationship and have moved on. Josh seemed to be enjoying his singlehood after all of the commotions. Josh, 38, has not updated his dating status as of 2018.
