The 10 Best Vuori Products of 2024

If you’ve found yourself wracking your brain for the name of that loungewear brand you just keep seeing on Instagram — you know, the one that everyone talks about that sells those super comfortable joggers — the brand you’re probably thinking of is Vuori.

Vuori's founder and CEO Joe Kudla first saw the opportunity to launch the company while living in San Diego. “A yogi, surfer, outdoorsman, and former college athlete, he was continually searching for the appropriate clothing to suit his own athletic endeavors, but found himself dissatisfied with the limited options available in the market,” Vuori's Chief Marketing Officer Nikki Sakelliou tells PEOPLE. “He drew upon his experiences to disrupt the market with a new perspective on performance apparel: functional, high-quality clothing that does not sacrifice style.” 

Vuori aims to sell clothing that is “built to move in” and provides stylish basics that will work with you throughout your everyday life. As Sakelliou says, “Vuori products are functionally designed to transcend traditional boundaries and blur the lines between fitness and life.” And, yes, they are famous for those super-soft, versatile joggers you’ve heard all your friends talk about, but the brand has plenty of other viral-worthy products, including its new outerwear offerings.

Aiming to provide these functional pieces to anyone and everyone who leads an active lifestyle, Vuori hopes to stand out in the market as something much more than just a brand, but rather “a way of life,” as Sakelliou says.

Keep reading for some of the brand’s most popular pieces and what makes them special.

Vuori Performance Jogger

Vuori

Yes, these are the infamous joggers that you haven’t stopped hearing about for what feels like two years now. Available in 17 colorways, it’s easy to imagine how you could create an entire collection of these joggers, which could easily transition from lounging and errands to beyond. It’s easy to picture them styled with an oversized hoodie and chunky socks for movie night at home, or with a crop top and leather jacket for a casual date night. What’s more, they have nearly 30,000 (yes, thirty thousand) reviews and an average rating of 4.8 stars. One reviewer even mentioned they had bought six pairs. Personally, any item that would inspire someone to buy six times over (especially when that item retails at $94) is worth checking out in our book.

Material: 89% recycled polyester, 11% elastane | Size Range: XXS-XXL | Colors: Black, Black Heather, Cosmo Heather, Heather Grey, Ruby Heather, and more

Vuori Canyon Insulated Jacket

Vuori

A lightweight insulated jacket is a must-have in transitional seasons like fall and spring — and the Canyon Insulated Jacket is versatile enough to wear with everything from your favorite pair of leggings to high-waisted jeans and your favorite fall boots. In addition to style and comfort, the jacket is also functional for weather and features a PFC-free DWR coating, giving it a water-resistant finish to protect you from the elements. 

Material: 85% recycled polyamide, 15% elastane; polyester/elastane lining | Size Range: XXS-XXL | Colors: Pistachio, hazel, black

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why is Vuori so popular?

    While Vuori might be known for their Insta-famous joggers, the reasons for the brand’s popularity goes beyond just one pair of pants. It’s often compared to Lululemon in terms of design, quality construction, and super-soft fabrics.

  • Are Vuori products worth it?

    If you're in the market for athleisurewear that can easily take you from the gym to the couch, Vuori won't disappoint. While not inexpensive, we believe they're a great value for the products. Similar styles from competitors are priced similar to or higher than Vuori's price point.

  • Where is Vuori brand from?

    Vuori is based in Encinitas, California, a place the brand continues to draw inspiration from. While very much a California brand, Vuori just opened a new East Coast flagship store in Manhattan. Other retail stores are located in California, Utah, Texas, Massachusetts, Arizona, Washington, Colorado, and London, England.

  • How do you wash Vuori joggers?

    If you invest almost $100 in a pair of sweatpants (albeit very nice sweatpants), you’re going to want to make sure you know how to wash and dry them properly (without ruining them). To safely wash Vuori Performance Joggers, the Vuori website suggests machine washing the pants on cold with like colors, tumble drying on low, and removing promptly. The website also states that the joggers should never be ironed.
