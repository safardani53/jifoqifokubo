Jersey Shore's Angelina Pivarnick 'leaked' costars Snooki, Jenni &amp; Deena's wedding toasts online to

JERSEY Shore’s Angelina Pivarnick “leaked” her costars Nicole ‘Snooki’ Polizzi, Jenni ‘JWoww’ Farley and Deena Cortese’s wedding toasts online in an effort to “make them look bad," a source has told The Sun.

According to a source Angelina, 35, told her now estranged husband Chris Larangeira that she was the one who secretly shared audio of her bridesmaids' over-the-top speeches at her own 2019 wedding to social media, which led fans to attack Nicole, Jenni and Deena.  

Until recently, Angelina's co-stars believed a wedding guest of the now estranged couple was to blame for the audio being released ahead of the scene airing on Jersey Shore: Family Vacation.

According to sources close to the estranged couple, the cast has been rocked by Chris' claims about Angelina's alleged confession.

One source said: “Angelina and Chris had a heads up about what these speeches were, and the general content of them.

"Everyone was totally on board with what was set to be said."

The speeches were full of zingers aimed at the bride.

Deena cheekily said: “Angelina, you're the lice to my hair."

Nicole added: “You're the fly to my s**t,” while Jenni said, “You are the trash to our bags.”

Jenni was even booed during her speech when she took a shot at Staten Island, where a majority of the guests were from. 

“You are the dump to our island,” Jenni told Angelina, in a clear reference to Staten Island’s Fresh Kills Landfill.  

The source claimed that Angelina leaked the audio of the toasts ahead of the episode's premiere without any context- and she knew it would make the other women "look bad.” 

Feeling 'duped'

Two years later, the MTV star’s fellow cast members feel "duped" and "hurt" by her supposed confession, the source claimed.

Although the source said that Chris' allegation has caused a serious rift among the female cast members, Angelina told The Sun that the allegations are totally untrue.

"It's my husband trying to sabotage me. He is walking around trying to sabotage me and to gain sympathy from strangers.

"He is trying to get me fired off the show with lies."

Shore-ly over for good 

According to his recent divorce filing, Chris, 42, moved out of the home Angelina purchased by herself in Freehold, New Jersey last year.  

Chris now lives in Staten Island, according to the complaint which was filed on January 20, and he stated that he and Angelina have “suffered irreconcilable differences which have caused a breakdown of the marriage for at least a period of six months."

In the filing, Chris also noted that there was “real and reasonable personal property that was acquired during the marriage that is a subject of equitable distribution.”   

As The Sun first exclusively reported, just over two years after the pair tied the knot in a lavish, drama-filled celebration in front of MTV cameras, Chris filed for divorce.  

Chris' filing comes less than a year after Angelina's divorce papers were dismissed, as the pair had reconciled over the summer of 2021.    

Right around the time Chris filed for divorce last month, Angelina had announced she was taking a break from social media to concentrate on her family.   

Angelina left fans concerned in recent social media posts, as they questioned the status of her marriage.   

Rumors of another split first circulated after she shared pics from her holiday gathering with Chris noticeably missing from the shots.  

She mentioned "life's little crazy right now for everyone" in her post, where she flashed a smile while standing alone in front of her brightly-lit Christmas tree.   

Back in August, The Sun revealed that Angelina’s divorce from Chris had been dismissed because neither of them followed up.
