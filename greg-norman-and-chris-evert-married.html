Greg Norman and Chris Evert: Married!

Greg Norman’s golf career was marked as much by major championship heartbreak as it was by prolific top-five finishes and earnings.

Yesterday should go a long way towards healing that broken heart.

It’s no green jacket, but Norman and Chris Evert, a 53-year-old tennis legend, were married Saturday in a sunset ceremony in the Bahamas.

On the same day that Ruben Studdard married Surata Zuri McCants, the athletic pair, both 53, exchanged vows in front of 140 family and friends, including Today host Matt Lauer and singer-songwriter Corey Hart.

Chris Evert and Greg Norman wed on Saturday in the Bahamas.

Evert’s youngest son Colton, 12, served as ring bearer, and sons Nick, 14, and Alex, 16, walked her down the aisle. Also present were Greg Norman’s daughter Morgan, 23, and son Gregory Jr., 21, who served as best man.

The wedding weekend kicked off on Friday with, natch, a golf and tennis tournament for guests. Wines from Greg Norman Estates (not half bad) were served at both the Friday rehearsal dinner and Saturday’s reception.

Chris Evert and Greg Norman got engaged December 9 while traveling in South Africa. This is the second marriage for both.
