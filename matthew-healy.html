Matthew Healy Hollywood Life

Bio:
Matthew Healy (born in London, England on April 8, 1989) is an English musician, best known as the lead singer and guitarist for the rock band The 1975. He met his bandmates in 2001 at Wilmslow High School in Manchester. He is credited with coming up with the band name. The 1975 has released four EPs, and their self-titled debut album, released on Sept. 2, 2013, debuted at No. 1 on the UK Albums Chart. The band has toured internationally since forming.
