In episode 2 The Tyger and the Lamb we finally learned about the group who rescued Rick Grimes (Andrew Lincoln) using a helicopter.

The group in question is called the Civic Republic, who form one of three communities that make up the Alliance of the Three.

The three-ringed symbol represents the peace agreement between the three biggest players in the apocalypse .

The Civic Republic spans the Southwestern United States, with strongolds in Omaha, Nebraska, and a currently unnamed society in Portland, Oregon.

They are supposedly developing a cure to the zombie plague, but to mistake them for pacifists would be a mistake.

The Civic Republic's military force slaughtered almost 10,000 people living inside Nebraska's Campus Colony, which used to be home to sisters Hope (Alexa Mansour) and Iris (Aliyah Royale), along with friends Elton (Nicolas Cantu) and Silas (Hal Cumpston).

The massacre, which unfolded towards the end of the show's premiere, was addressed by Civic Republic Military (CRM) soldier Barca (Al Calderon).

While the official stance is that the Civic Republic "neutralised a threat," Barca shows remorse about the attack.
