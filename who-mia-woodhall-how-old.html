Who is Mia Woodhall and how old is she?

SOCIAL media has helped launch some of the well-known internet stars we all know and love today.

One of those is Mia Woodhall - here's everything you need to know.

Who is Mia Woodhall?

Mia Woodhall is a fashion model, Instagram influencer, and social media personality.

Born on June 8, 2002, she grew up in Malaga, Spain.

Her father, Sean Woodhall, was a businessman who sadly passed away in 2008.

Mia's social media career kicked off when she joined TikTok in 2020.

Many users of the app were fascinated by her beauty, and she has since gained a large following.

In December 2022, she made headlines after revealing she spent half a grand trying to pass her car theory test.

"It took me 24 times to get my theory test," she wrote in the TikTok video's caption.

But in a twist of fate, she revealed she passed her practical test the first time.

Who is Mia Woodhall dating?

Mia is currently in a relationship with Luke Skelton.

Not much is known about Luke, as she tends to keep details about her relationship private.

However, on her Instagram page, she has a story highlights dedicated to him captioned with a heart emoji.

The pair recently spent valentines day in Paris together where he decorated the hotel room with red balloons and 100 roses.

She captioned the Instagram story: "he did good".

What does Mia Woodhall post on social media?

Mia's Instagram consists of bikini photos and other trendy outfits.

As of March 2023, the internet personality has over 640,000 followers.

She has promoted products from companies such as Labels by Lux, Mars the Label, and TH clothing.
