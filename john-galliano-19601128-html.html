John Charles Galliano is an English fashion designer, serving as the lead designer for French fashion houses Givenchy, Christian Dior and private label John Galliano. Currently, Galliano is the creative director of fashion house Maison Margiela in Paris. Galliano has been honored four times as British Designer of the Year. In a 2004 BBC poll, he was named the fifth most influential person in British culture.
