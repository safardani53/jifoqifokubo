Hailey Baldwin, Miley Cyrus &amp; More Hollywood Life

If there’s one fabric that’s never going out of style, it’s denim. To elevate the look of a basic pair of jeans, or a casual jean jacket, some of our favorite stars have reached for double-denim ensembles — or-triple denim, in Bella Hadid‘s case. The 24-year-old model was photographed leaving a New York City hair salon in denim jeans and a vest, topped off by a long-sleeved denim shirt, on Feb. 27, 2021. She wore a brown turtleneck underneath the denim ensemble (a great layering trick to balance out all that jeans material) as well as a plaid face mask to switch up the fabrics.

Lori Harvey also demonstrated how to rock denim-on-denim! The stunner stepped out in dramatic floor sweeping white fur coat while shopping in SoHo on February 15, 2021. Lori sported a long, denim button-down top with a pair of denim pants that gave us major ’90s vibes! Along with her full hair and makeup, Lori’s fun fashion sense came through effortlessly with her fuzzy white coat as she did some shopping in New York City on the blisteringly chilly day. Vanessa Hudgens also channeled the ’90s (and an episode of Melrose Place) with her Canadian Tuxedo on Saturday, November 28, 2020. Vanessa rocked a loose fitting pair of jeans and a sherpa-lined denim jacket as she enjoyed a Blue Bottle coffee in Los Angeles. The star was out with her BFF GG Magree and it appeared they did some shopping at Urban Outfitters. Back in October, Hailey Baldwin rocked a head-to-toe denim look when she stepped out with her husband Justin Bieber following a meal at an upscale West Hollywood restaurant. She opted for light wash denim jeans, an ab-bearing black sports bra, and a matching light wash jean jacket. Singer Miley Cyrus also made a fall fashion statement with her look on October 2, when she stepped out in New York City wearing a head-turning ‘fit. The “Prisoner” singer reached for a pair of boot cut jeans and an oversized jacket in the same shade of denim. Of course, she didn’t stop there. Miley also accessorized with black boots, an over-the-top black hat, and a matching protective face mask. What a look! Emily Ratajkowski also tried the trend when she was out in New York City on March 17. She threw on a pair of high-waisted, light-wash, straight-leg jeans with a fitted cropped Inamorata Elysian top in black. On top of her shirt, she donned an oversized light-wash Levi‘s Type 3 Faux Shearling Trucker Jacket. Emily accessorized her look with a pair of white Nike Air Max Sneakers, high socks, Vera Wang Grace Sunglasses, and Jennifer Fisher Mini Lilly Hoop Earrings. Wearing denim on denim is cute and casual. But when added with heels and full hair and makeup, the look is perfect for a night out. Whether it’s a jean mini skirt, a jumpsuit, overalls, or a chambray shirt, this look works in so many different ways. This is a trend that will never go out of style.
