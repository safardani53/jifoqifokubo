Is Ashley McBryde single? | The US Sun

COUNTRY music artist Ashley McBryde has kept her personal life very private over the years and doesn’t often share information about her relationships.

Her rise to fame and fortune begs the question of whether anyone has stolen the singer’s heart.

Who is Ashley McBryde?

Originally from Hardy, Arkansas, McBryde got her start when she released her self-titled album in 2006.

Not to be confused with fellow country artist Martina McBride, Ashley McBryde had started her career on her own having independently produced her first album.

However, it wasn’t until McBryde dropped her 2018 album, Girl Going Nowhere, that she received several Grammy nominations.

Girl Going Nowhere was nominated at the 61st Grammy Award ceremony for Best Country Album. She received two other nominations related to the album in 2019 for Best Country Solo Performance and Best Country Song.

Her next album, Never Will, was nominated for a Grammy in 2020 for Best Country Album, but McBryde has yet to secure the award.

Is Ashley McBryde dating anyone?

McBryde has shared little about her life over the years, including her relationship status. Her past relationships are unknown, but she is currently not married and is single.

What is Ashley McBryde up to now?

At the 2021 CMA Awards, McBryde paired up with Carly Pearce to sing an emotional duet for Never Wanted to Be That Girl.

The singers co-wrote the song that is on Pearce’s newest album, 29: Written in Stone. Pearce released the album on September 17 but released the single two days earlier.

While some responded on Twitter that the song was “too emotional” or “too dramatic,” others gushed over McBryde and Pearce’s performance.

One person tweeted, “Awesome performance with Carly Pearce last night!! What a great song but even better seeing it live!

"Love your music. Always such a real, gutsy raw vibe.”

Carly Pearce ultimately won the Female Vocalist of the Year award at the 55th CMA Awards on November 10, 2021.
