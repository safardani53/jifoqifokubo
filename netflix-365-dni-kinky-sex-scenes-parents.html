Netflix fans horrified after accidentally watching 365 DNIs kinky sex scenes with their PARENTS i

VIEWERS of saucy Netflix film 365 DNI have been left cringing after watching the sexy scenes by mistake - with their parents.

Like the leading lady, the Polish flick has gone down a storm with its ultra rude scenes of a sexual nature - including her performing oral sex on her co-star character on a plane.

Fans haven't been able to believe their eyes and have been left shocked and embarrassed - especially after watching it with their parents.

One viewer took to Twitter to vent: "MY PARENTS ARE WATCHING 365 DNI IN OUR LIVING ROOM. HELP."

Another agreed: "Can you imagine watching and then your parents come sit down and watch it too."

One fan suggested that you should never watch with anyone else - except a partner.

They said: "But for real, don’t watch this movie with your parents, kids, roommate, friends, babysitter... watch it alone or with your significant other cause, whewww!"

Another fan joked: "My parents just started watching. This is going to be comedy."

One social media user wrote: "I’m not into spoilers but I’ll say one thing... don’t watch with your parents lol."

A concerned fan shared: "I think my parents just started 365 DNI in the living room. Poor people, they don’t know what the movie is about."

Another agreed: "365 DNI on Netflix is not one to watch with your parents."

One keen viewer stated: "I’ve watched 365 DNI twice and my parents were just about to watch it... I just sat there like pls pls pls pick something else."

One red-faced fan wrote: "I saw 365 DNI trending on Netflix so I casually put it on the living room TV.... y’all know I’m at my parents house... boy."

Other comments included: "I've watched 365 DNI together with my parents... yep, it literally felt like I was watching porn with my parents. Never again."

And a warning: "Do not watch 365 DNI on Netflix with your parents or family."

The raunchy flick has shocked fans by a graphic X-rated oral sex scene.

Fans were left crimson as they watched the air hostess perform fellatio on her co-star.

Many viewers of the Polish flick, released this week, moaned they were left feeling "sexually frustrated".

It came as the movie, centred on a member of the Sicilian Mafia who kidnaps a sales director, is jam-packed with racy sex scenes, as well as voyeurism and BDSM, which many have likened to 50 Shades of Grey.

Some were left thinking the scenes were that realistic that the the actors were actually having sex.

The Polish romantic drama dropped on Netflix in the UK on June 7, 2020.

365 Days (365 DNI) is a Polish erotic romantic drama based on Blanka Lipinska's novel of the same name.

Written and directed by Barbara Bialowas the official synopsis reads: "Massimo is a member of the Sicilian Mafia family and Laura is a sales director.

"She does not expect that on a trip to Sicily trying to save her relationship, Massimo will kidnap her and give her 365 days to fall in love with him."

While trying to do so, the pair engage in a number of steamy romps in a multitude of locations, including a boat.

Dubbed as Polish cinema's answer to Fifty Shades of Grey, the sexy film based on Blanka Lipinska's novel of the same name will certainly spice up lockdown viewing.
