Erika Jayne Posts Naked Picture, Feels Ire Of Internet

Erika Jayne loves to be the focal point of any occasion. That much has been made abundantly clear on several different occasions. 

If you watch The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills online, you will probably be thinking of the fallout from that time she went to a party with no underwear on. 

This time, however, the reality TV star decided to let the internet see her goodies. Yes, she went the full way and unveiled a naked picture to her followers on Instagram. 

Have a look at the picture below, which is most definitely not safe for work. 

"Mikey made me do it … Miami nights," she captioned the photo, which was very heavily edited. Maybe she was making good use of the Snapchat filters. 

As you can probably expect, the internet was not entirely overwhelmed at Erika’s use of social media to flaunt her assets. 

That did not faze her, and she later updated the caption to school the haters on some key information they were missing out in their comments.

Her updated caption reads as follows: 

1. This is my pre DWTS body? 2. I am not currently in Miami but am in NYC 3. I posted this knowing most of you would be judgmental and couldn’t resist a chance to tear me down and I was right. 4. Go be offended at your own life. 5. I’m living my life to the fullest. ❤️

It is good that she doesn’t care what people think, but the timing of the picture could not be more hilarious. She posted it on Tuesday night right after the conclusion of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills’ reunion extravaganza.

The reunion episodes have had a huge focus on Erika showing off her goodies to Dorit’s husband, Paul,  earlier in the season, and the lasting effect it had on her.  

It sure seems like it was a big middle finger to the haters and, of course, Dorit, but we’ll need to wait until the cameras start rolling again to know whether this is a new feud. 

Erika was recently eliminated from ABC’s hit reality series, Dancing with the Stars, in which she was partnered with Gleb Savchenko. 

She made headlines for her racy performances but wanted everyone to know that she did not care what they thought of them.
