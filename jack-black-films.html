Who is Jack Black? | The US Sun

ACTOR Jack Black has amassed a huge fortune over his almost 30 year career. 

Here is what we know about Jack Black, one of Hollywood’s most successful comedic actors. 

Who is Jack Black? 

Jack Black is an actor and musician who has starred in blockbuster hits including School Of Rock and The Holiday.  

He has worked as a voice actor, director and producer. 

Born in California in 1969, Black had a troubled upbringing, his parents divorced when he was ten and he started struggling with drug abuse at the age of 14. 

In 1989, Black’s brother died of Aids. 

Black was accepted into the college UCLA, but dropped out to pursue a career in Hollywood. 

The 51-year-old is now a much beloved actor and musician.

What is Jack Black’s net worth? 

Jack is worth about $50million. 

He broke on to the Hollywood scene in the 2000 film High Fidelity. 

He has since starred in blockbuster smashes including School Of Rock, The Holiday, Gulliver’s Travels and Jumanji. 

He was nominated for a Golden Globe for his work in School Of Rock and Bernie, and has a star on the Hollywood Walk of fame. 

As a voice actor, Jack has worked on familiar favourites like Kung Fu Panda, Shark Tank and Ice Age. 

His starring roles in the two billion-dollar franchises, Kung Fu Panda and Jumanji, have added the most to his net worth. 

Jack is also the lead vocalist of the band Tenacious D. 

The grammy-award winning comedic rock band have released four albums and a film.

  • High Fidelity (2000)

  • Goosebumps (2015)
  • Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle (2017)

Is Jack Black married? 

Jack Black is married to Tanya Haden. 

The pair met in 2006 and married that same year. 

Haden is an American artist, musician and singer.

She is a triplet, and daughter of Jazz bassist Charlie Haden, and starred in a band with her sisters called, The Haden Triplets.

The two mostly keep their private life away from the public eye, but in an interview with The Sun in 2013, Black revealed that he waited 15 years to ask out his now wife. 

The pair met as teenagers at school, but Black was too nervous to make his move. 

When he does discuss his personal life, he raves about his wife, calling her talented and beautiful.
