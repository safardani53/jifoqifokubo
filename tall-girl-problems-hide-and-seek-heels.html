Im 67 &amp; dont fit in bed, people play hide and seek behind me in public &amp; say I shouldnt

A VERY tall woman has hit back at trolls who believe she shouldn’t wear heels in public and shared the realities of being 6’7. 

Natalia Veru is a former professional basketball player who continued to grow in height until the age of 21. 

Since then, she’s had some interesting experiences when it comes to her height - from not fitting in her own bed to bending down to get through doors. 

In one video, she showed herself being interviewed in public outside, when a man came up from behind her to play hide and seek with his pals. 

“That’s how people react to me in public,” she wrote over this. 

In another clip, she revealed that people often tell her not to wear heels or she’ll be “too tall” in public.

After staring quizzically at her block heeled boots, she then changed the scene to her walking through cobbled streets - appearing to tower over everyone around her.

“Being the tallest woman in the city,” she wrote over this, before confirming that she’s 7 foot tall in the heels in another clip. 

Meanwhile, in a third video, she asked: “Do you mind a woman being taller?” 

Posing in several outfits before bending down to get through a door into an upstairs room.

She could then be seen standing at the back of a crowd, where she lifted her arms to take a picture of what was happening at the front. 

Another segment showed her with her feet dangling off the end of a bed, unable to fit into it completely. 

While she might get mixed reactions from the public, people in the comments loved her height and couldn’t help but gush over her. 

One person wrote: “As a 5ft woman, I have nothing but admiration for you!”

Another shared: “Honestly, same! After all, not everyday people get to witness a tall and beautiful woman.”

A third posted: “hold your head up high be proud,” followed by a clapping hands emoji.

“Height doesn’t mean anything. Are you a good person,? That’s what matters,” a fourth said. 

While a fifth added: “Idc much about height. She just got to be beautiful inside and out.”
