Man dies and teenage girl fighting for life after three-car crash

A MAN has died and a teenage girl is fighting for her life after a horror three-car smash.

Emergency services rushed to the scene on Auchenhowie Road in East Dunbartonshire, Scotland, at around 9pm last night.

A 21-year-old man who was a backseat passenger in a Skoda Superb died at the scene.

While the teenager, 16, who was in the backseat with him is in a critical condition at the Queen Elizabeth Hospital in Glasgow.

Cops confirmed the 19-year-old driver was also seriously injured and is being treated in the same hospital.

Three men have been arrested after the shocking crash as police continue to investigate.

Two other passengers, men aged 23 and 17, sustained minor injuries.

No one in the other two cars involved, an Audi A4 and a Ford Tourneo Classic, was injured.

Pictures showed recovery teams taking away the crashed Skoda which was ran off the road.

Cops have urged anyone with information to come forward after the fatal incident.

"Information can be passed to police via 101. Please quote reference number 3566 of Sunday February 26 2023, when calling."
