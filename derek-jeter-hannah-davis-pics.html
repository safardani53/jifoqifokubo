Photos Of The Couple Hollywood Life

Look back at photos of gorgeous couple, Derek Jeter and Hannah Davis, throughout their relationship.

Congrats are in order for Derek Jeter & Hannah Davis who  welcomed a son, Kaius Green Jeter on May 5, 2023. Derek Jeter’s love life was wildly speculated abut throughout his entire baseball career. Despite high profile relationships with Mariah Carey, Minka Kelly, Vanessa Minnillo, Jordana Brewster, Vida Guerra and more, Derek didn’t find the love of his life until 2012, when he was in his late 30s. He began dating model Hannah Davis, who is 16 years his junior, that year. They were engaged by 2015, after Derek officially retired from baseball.

In July 2016, Derek and Hannah tied the knot, and did not waste any time when it came to having kids. The pair’s first daughter, Bella, was born in Aug. 2017, followed by another little girl, Story, just over a year later in Jan. 2019. Their third daughter was born in December 2021. The family is extremely private, and Derek and Hannah make sure that their kids are mostly kept out of the public eye.
