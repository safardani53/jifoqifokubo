11 Aldi must-buy items this week starting at $3.50 - including a weighted blanket and cute Christmas

A BARGAIN-HUNTING influencer has shared her favorite holiday gifting items from Aldi, including Christmas-themed treats perfect for parties.

The large retailer offers a wide range of items from toys to foods to desserts - and this TikToker took to the platform to reveal 11 that start at just $3.50.

Known on TikTok as phillyfoodies, the shopper took a trip to Aldi and found a bunch of products that attract a large audience.

Many of the foods she found were Christmas-themed, such as Christmas tree-shaped bread or ice cream.

Below are her top picks.

Food Items

  • Aldi currently has star-shaped and Christmas-tree-shaped brioche bread
  • Pizza: this also comes in Christmas shapes

Christmas treats

Even though it's getting colder, you can never be too full for desserts.

The Sundae Shoppe Snowflake Cones cost $3.49 for four cones per box.

Each cone is wrapped in blue paper and topped with a small plastic cap to help the ice cream keep its snowflake shape intact.  

Aldi also has gluten-free gingerbread donuts for a limited time.

The gluten-free donuts cost $4.49 for six donuts per box and can be found in the Aldi Find freezers.

The last two items she found were:

  • Reindeer pops
  • Snowman pops

Each box has four pops.

The reindeer pops consist of caramel, chocolate, and strawberry-flavored ice cream with chocolate drops for the reindeer's eyes.

The snowman pops consist of vanilla and strawberry-flavored ice cream.
