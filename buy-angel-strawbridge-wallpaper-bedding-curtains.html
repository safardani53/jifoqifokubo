Can I buy Angel Strawbridge's wallpaper, bedding, curtains and fabric?

FANS of Escape to the Chateau are keen to know where they can buy Angel Strawbridge's designs from.

Here's everything you need to know about the Channel 4 star's products.

Where can I buy Angel Strawbridge's wallpapers and fabrics?

'The Chateau' range is available to buy in various shops.

Angel's range includes wallpapers, cushions, bedding, curtains and fabrics.

They are inspired by The Chateau de la Motte Husson, the chateau Angel owns with her husband Dick Strawbridge.

The 43-year-old's work includes her 'Museum' wallpaper, which is made from off-cuts found in her chateau's attic when she and Dick bought the place.

Shops that sell 'The Chateau' range include Next, Just Fabrics and Wallpaper Direct.

How much do Angel's designs cost?

Angel's products vary in price.

The linen cushions cost around £30 each - the same price as a 32cm role of the wallpaper.

A duvet and pillowcase set will set you back between £45 to £70, depending on what size you purchase.

And the curtains are priced between £66 and £117.

What is her business The Vintage Patisserie?

Angel also runs The Vintage Patisserie - a hospitality business.

She initially appeared on Dragon's Den in 2010 to promote the idea and was offered a £100,000 investment from Deborah Meaden and Theo Paphitis.

Although the investment never materialised, the business opened a year later in Hackney, London.

When Angel and Dick relocated to France in 2015, so did The Vintage Patisserie.

The business offers wedding packages - held at the couple's chateau - afternoon tea and 'unique events'.
