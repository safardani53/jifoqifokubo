Who is Elliot Sloan:

Elliot Sloan is a famous Skateboarder. He was born on July 29, 1988 and his birthplace is New York City, NY. Elliot is also well known as, Skateboarder who won gold at the 2013 and 2017 Summer X Games in Los Angeles, competing in the Skateboard Big Air division. He has over 60,000 followers on his elliotsloan Instagram account.

Elliot is originated from United States. His greatest influence in skateboarding was Tony Hawk.
