Pam Shriver (Tennis Player) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Pamela Howard Shriver is a former professional American tennis player. She is currently a tennis host for ESPN and an expert for BBC tennis radio. During the 1980s and 1990s, she won 133 titles, including 21 women's singles titles, 111 doubles titles and one doubles title. In the Grand Slam awards, Shriver won 22 titles, 21 doubles titles and one doubles title. She also won the women's doubles gold medal at the 1988 Seoul Olympics with Zina Garrison as a teammate. Shriver and her regular doubles partner Martina Navratilova are the only female couple to have completed the Grand Slam in one calendar year, winning all four major titles in 1984 .
