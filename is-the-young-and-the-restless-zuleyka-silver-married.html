Is The Young and the Restless star Zuleyka Silver married?

ZUKEYKA Silver has starred on The Young and the Restless since September 2022.

Now, fans want to know if the soap star is married.

Who is Zuleyka Silver?

Silver was born on August 2, 1991, in Tijuana, Mexico.

The 31-year-old actress and model joined The Young and the Restless cast in September 2022.

According to her IMDb profile, she has appeared in a number of other TV shows, such as Too Old to Die Young, The Late Late Show with James Corden, and Real Husbands of Hollywood.

As per her CBS Biography, Silver was also featured in 90210, Jimmy Kimmel Live!, The Strip, Hawaii 5-0, The Mentalist, Young Sheldon, and Code Black.

Her Biography also states that she is "a first-generation Mexican American" who earned her bachelor's degree in philosophy from UCLA. She minored in film, television, and digital media.

It also states that she is a "Meisner and Adler technique-trained actress" who studied in New York City, New York and Los Angeles, California.

It continues: "In her spare time, Zuleyka enjoys salsa dancing, hiking, reading, and spending time with her cat, Hemingway."

Silver has also accumulated a large social media following, with 175K followers on her Instagram page, @zuleykasilver.

Who is Audra Charles?

Since September 2022, Silver has played the character Audra Charles on The Young and the Restless.

As per Silver's CBS Biography, Charles is a "a successful businesswoman" who "arrives in Genoa City with a secret agenda and a mysterious past."

Soaps in Depth states: "Audra Charles introduced herself to Nate Hastings in Crimson Lights when she recognized him. The former doctor thought she was one of his old patients, but she explained that she had been hired by Jill Abbott to help take Chancellor-Winters public.

"The pair met again the next day at Society where Audra asked Nate to give her the behind-the-scenes scoop at the company so she would know exactly who she was dealing with. "

Is Zuleyka Silver married?

While Silver does not appear to be married, she reportedly has a boyfriend.

According to Model Fact, she has been dating a man named Javier Huerta since 2018.

However, there is little public knowledge on Huerta and their reported relationship.
