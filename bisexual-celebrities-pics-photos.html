There are many different kinds of sexual orientations out there. Really, so many! There is lesbian, gay, bisexual, pansexual, and so much more.

Bisexuality may have much stigma around it and might not be the most understood, but that does not mean that there are not still many proud bisexuals out there! Bisexual people have both a romantic attraction and sexual attraction toward males and females, or a person who is attracted to more than one (aka two) sexes or genders. The “bi” (just like the prefix “di”) means “two”… hence the term “bisexuality.” Furthermore, bisexual people do not necessarily equate to equal sexual attraction to both men and women; but their sexual attraction can be rather unequal toward the two sexes.
