Im an ex Bath &amp; Body Works employee - store's return policy could save you a lot of money on prod

BATH & Body Works has arguably one of the best returns policies out there, an ex-employee has revealed.

Rachel who goes by beyoutiful_mess_of3 on TikTok is a former Bath & Body Works employee and loves to share with her nearly 22,000 followers shopping hacks from her time at the company.

In a recent video, Rachel explains how shoppers can exchange absolutely anything at the large retailer - no questions asked.

This may not be a huge secret since they wear a pin stating 100% guarantee.

However, many people might not know that returns apply to any product, no matter the year or usage. 

For example, burnt candles you've used, nearly empty lotion bottles, sprays, soaps, you name it, the company will take it back.

To return the products, you just head to the cashier and exchange them item for item.

You can tell them why you didn't like the product, but Rachel says they won't ask questions, they will just let you exchange them. 

What happens to used products? 

After the TikTok went viral, Rachel posted another video explaining where the used products go when they get returned, and it might surprise you.

If the product was brand new, never been used, and something they are currently still selling, Bath & Body Works will put the product back on the shelves and resell it.

If it’s a products that has been used, or an older item that is no longer in the store, they will damage the product and throw it out.

Rachel says that at her store, whenever they damaged the products and took the garbage out, employees had to compact it at least three times to make sure that each item was damaged enough.

This would need to be done so no one digging through their trash would be able to use the products once it’s thrown out.
