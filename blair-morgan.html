Who is Blair Morgan:

Blair Morgan is a famous Motorcycle Racer. He was born on October 9, 1975 and his birthplace is Canada. Blair is also well known as, Motocross rider from Canada known for being a two-time CMRC National Motocross champion. He is also a prominent snocross racer, having won five gold medals at the X-Games.

Blair is originated from Canada. He and Jolene Van Vugt are both champion motocross riders born in Canada.
