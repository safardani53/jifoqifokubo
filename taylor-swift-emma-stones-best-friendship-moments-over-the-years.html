Taylor Swift, Emma Stone's Best Friendship Moments Over the Years

Taylor Swift and Emma Stone have remained one of Hollywood’s closest pairs of celebrity besties over the years.

The two first met at the 2008 Young Hollywood Awards. “I listened to some of her music, and I wrote her an e-mail saying I liked her music, I swear,” Stone told MTV News of their friendship’s early days in April 2010. “And then we started talking and hanging out.”

Swift and Stone have done everything from grabbing ice cream together to supporting each other at premieres and concerts.

While Swift has built strong connections with several famous faces — including the HAIM sisters, Lorde, Ed Sheeran and more — her friendship with Stone is one she doesn’t take for granted.
