Who is Cardi Bs husband, Offset? The US Sun

OFFSET is a US rapper who rose to fame in the hip-hop world with his former group, Migos.

Off stage, the artist is often seen with his wife and fellow rapper, Cardi B, and his children.

Who is Cardi B's husband, Offset?

Offset is a US rapper born Kiari Kendrell Cephus on December 14, 1991.

Alongside Takeoff and Quavo, he was the third member of the hip-hop group Migos before leaving the group in early 2022.

Migos was formed in 2008 and has released three studio albums.

Yung Rich Nation (2015), Culture (2017), and their 2018 release Culture II have all enjoyed chart success.

Culture II reached No4 with Culture peaking at No16 in the UK Album chart.

Their number one Billboard hit Bad And Boujee was released in 2016 and featured Lil Uzi Vert but only reached No30 in the UK.

They have also had three other top ten US hits - Motor Sport, with Nicki Minaj and Cardi B, Stir Fry, which peaked at number eight, and Walk It, Talk It featuring Drake. In the UK they've had one top 10 hits with the Calvin Harris song Slide.

On November 1, 2022, Takeoff died at age 28 from a tragic shot in Houston.

Offset previously reached No5 in the UK Singles chart featuring Tyga's song, Taste. His 2019 debut solo album, Father of 4, reached No19 on the UK charts.

He has over 23 million monthly listeners on Spotify.

How many children does Offset have?

The Georgia native has five children - Jordan, Kody, Kalea, Kulture, and Wave Set Cephus - whom he shares with four different baby mamas.

He and his wife, Cardi B, welcomed their first child together - a daughter, Kulture - on July 10, 2018.

Their second child - son, Wave Set Cephus - was born on September 4, 2021.

Offset's other baby mamas include Justine Watson, Oriel Jamie, and Shya L’Amour.

When did Offset and Carbi get married?

Cardi B and Offset started dating in January 2017 and their love story has been complicated.

Cardi said they first met at an industry event and Offset pursued her. "He was very consistent," Cardi said in a video on her X, formerly known as Twitter, account.

The pair got married in secret on September 30, 2017, and tied the knot in Atlanta, Georgia, in their apartment with only Cardi B's cousin as the witness, per Brides.

Their marriage was kept secret until TMZ revealed the news in June 2018.

After their marriage, Offset proposed to Cardi B during a concert in Philadelphia on October 27, 2017.

"There are so many moments that I share with the world and there are moments that I want to keep for myself! Getting married was one of those moments! Our relationship was so new breaking up and making up and we had a lot of growing to do but we was so in love we didn't want to lose each other," tweeted Cardi B.
