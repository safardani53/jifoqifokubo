When is Black Friday 2023?

Black Friday is always on the final Friday in November, which this year falls on November 24, that's this Friday.

The sale event follows the US celebrations of Thanksgiving, which is on the fourth Thursday of the month.

Black Friday sales often start ahead of the official date though, so make sure to check out your favourite websites in the days and weeks leading up to the big shopping day.

When does DeWalt's Black Friday sale start?

Since Dewalt doesn't have its own store, you'll have to wait until retailers that store the tools begin their Black Friday sales which is this Friday, the 24th of November.

Luckily for anyone who wants to begin looking around now, a few retailers, such as Amazon and John Lewis, have begun early Black Friday deals, allowing you more time to find the best prices or pick up items early if you don't want to wait around.

Is Dewalt taking part in Black Friday this year?

Yes, although not directly. Dewalt doesn't have its own online shop, so you will have to look for deals at well-known stockists.

Major retailers such as Amazon, B&Q, Toolstation, and ScrewFix always take part in Black Friday sales, meaning you can find fantastic Dewalt deals there.

We saw a wide selection of tools and DIY essentials feature in the discounts last year, and we are certain many similar items with feature in price cuts again for 2023.

When does DeWalt's Black Friday sale end?

As mentioned previously, DeWalt not having an actual store means you'll have to keep an eye out for when retailers that stock the products plan to end their Black Friday sales.

While it is possible that some of them will extend the sales so they cover the weekend, it's not a guarantee, so ensure if you see something you're after, you pick it up on Black Friday to avoid the risk of missing out.
