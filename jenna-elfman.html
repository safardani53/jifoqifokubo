Jenna Elfman Biography

Golden Globes, USA winner for Best Performance by an Actress in a TV-Series - Comedy/Musical, Jenna Elfman is one of the famous actresses in the United States and she is popular for portraying her role in the television series, Dharma & Greg.

Jenna Elfman Childhood, Education

Born as Jennifer Mary Butala in Los Angeles, California, the United States of America to Sue Grace Butala and Richard Wayne Butala, Jenna was raised in an upper-middle-class family. She is an American and is of Croatian descent.

Elfman did her high school from the Los Angeles County High School for the Arts and later studied at California State University, Northridge (CSUN). Besides, as a child, at the age of 5, she was also trained in ballet. However, later, after the tendon came off her bone, she was bound to leave ballet.

Jenna Elfman Husband & Marriage Details

In February 1991 when Jenna was at a Sprite commercial audition, she met the love of her life, Bodhi Elfman. Bodhi Elfman is also an American actor.

They fell in love and began their love affairs since then. They finally got married on the 18th of February 1995.

Professional Details, Career as an Actor

The graduate of Los Angeles County High School for the Arts, Jenna joined California State University soon after her high school graduation.

As her childhood dreams were acting and dancing, she began her career as a dancer and that was in the music video, Halo.

In 1992, she appeared as a ballet dancer in Murder, She Wrote and then in 1997, she played her first feature film role.

Salary & Net worth

In 2009, for her role in Accidentally on Purpose, she earned the salary of $125,000 per episode. Her net worth is said to be around $16 million.
