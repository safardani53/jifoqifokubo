What is the best underwear for running?

Underwear for running is supposed to be tight and form-fitting in order to be supportive and to prevent chafing one of your most delicate areas, but breathable where you need it to be.

The best underwear for running will also have lining made from materials like cotton that will absorb the sweat and microbes responsible for infections.

So if you're sold on the idea that pants for running will transform your training, check our guide on the best running underwear out there.

Do runners wear underwear?

Runners don't always wear underwear.

Running shorts often have a special lining designed to reduce friction and to keep you dry, so you don't need to wear anything underneath.
