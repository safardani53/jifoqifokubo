Mayumi Tanaka is a Japanese actress, voice actress and narrator. She voices Ryuunosuke Fujinami in Urusei Yatsura, Koenma from Yu Yu Hakusho, Pazu from Laputa: Castle in the Sky, Monkey D. Luffy from One Piece, Kuririn, Yajirobe and Uranai Baba in Dragon Ball, Kirimaru Settsuno from Nintama Rantarō, Kanna Kirishima in the Sakura Wars series; TwinBee's main role in the shooting game series of Konami TwinBee and MegaMan Volnutt in the Mega Man Legends series and its related Capcom crossovers. She received the Kazue Takahashi Award at the 5th Seiyu Awards.
