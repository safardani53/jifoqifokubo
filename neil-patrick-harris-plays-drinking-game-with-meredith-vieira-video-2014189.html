Neil Patrick Harris Plays Drinking Game With Meredith Vieira: Video

Bottoms up! Neil Patrick Harris had a little bit too much fun while appearing on The Meredith Vieira Show on Thursday, Sept. 18. The Gone Girl actor played a round of “Drink or Dish” with the daytime talk show host, in which he had to take a shot of white wine whenever he refused to answer a personal question.

“Tell us a time when you used your fame when you really shouldn’t have — you abused it,” Vieira asked the newlywed. Straying away from the rules, Harris, 41, instead chugged the rest of the bottle of wine. “No comment,” he quipped.
