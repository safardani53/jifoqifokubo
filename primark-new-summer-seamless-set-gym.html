Shoppers go wild for new seamless gym sets in Primark hailed as only fit you need for summer

IF you shop in Primark then there's a good chance you're already a fan of their seamless sets.

And the bargain store has pulled it out of the bag for summer with a new gym collection.

The mint green pieces are perfect for the warmer weather with prices starting from just £4.50.

Bosses at Primark's branch in East Kilbride, Lanarkshire, shared a TikTok clip of the new stock yesterday.

And they hailed the range as "the only gym set you need for summer."

The summer gym range features £8 seamless leggings, matching shorts and top priced at £4.50 each, and even a mint green sports bra (£6).

The Primark store's video has already racked up more than 17,000 views.

And shoppers are going wild for the new pieces.

One gushed: "Need omg."

A second wrote: "What I’m getting today."
