Pregnant Olivia Wilde Wears Red Caftan Dress on Red Carpet

Scarlet siren! Olivia Wilde bundled her baby bump in a red caftan dress on Wednesday, September 21, in NYC, at an event that honored Martin Scorsese with the Friars Club’s Entertainment Icon Award.

The Vinyl actress, 32, who is expecting her second child with partner Jason Sudeikis, stunned in the strawberry-red design by Valentino. She contrasted the style with Steve Madden’s blue velvet Gonzo platform sandals ($90, stevemadden.com) and finished with gold hoop earrings and a ring by Vita Fede.

Wilde confirmed her pregnancy in April via Instagram, and at the event, she told Us she was feeling “great!”
