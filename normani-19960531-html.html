Normani (Pop Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Normani Kordei Hamilton is an American singer and dancer. She auditioned as a solo act for the American television series The X Factor in 2012, after which she became a member of the girl group Fifth Harmony, which went on to become one of the best-selling girl groups of all time. While in Fifth Harmony, Normani competed in Dancing with the Stars and released her first song outside of the group "Love Lies", a duet with Khalid which peaked in the top ten in the US and was certified 5× platinum by RIAA. Following Fifth Harmony's indefinite hiatus, Normani released a two-song EP with Calvin Harris, Normani x Calvin Harris. That same year, she released the single "Waves" featuring 6lack, which won Normani the MTV Video Music Award for Best R&B Video. Her duet with Sam Smith "Dancing with a Stranger", reached the top ten in the UK and US. In August 2019, Normani released her first solo single, "Motivation".
