Christian Siriano Biography

American fashion designer Christian Siriano who is also one of the members of the Council of Fashion Designers of America became popular in the fashion world after winning the American design competition show Project Runway.

Childhood, And Education

Born as Christian Siriano in Annapolis, Maryland, the United States of America to Peter Siriano, Christian was raised in a middle-class family. He is an American and is of Italian and German descent.
