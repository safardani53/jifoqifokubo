Who Is Matina Kyrou, Jordan Kyrou Sister? Age And Wiki

Jordan Kyrou’s sister, Matina Kyrou, has been an avid fan and supporter of the NHL career of her brother.

While Matina is not directly into ice hockey, she actively attends her brother’s games. A Tiktok star and Instagram model, she often features Jordan on her posts.

She is the only one of three siblings who didn’t dip her toes into the rink. But having said that, it also won’t be wrong to say that Matina’s support for her brothers is unparalleled.

Jordan, a Canadian-born ice hockey right winger plays for the NHL team St. Louis Blues (2017- present). 

The Blues chose him as the second-round (35th overall) pick in the 2016 NHL Entry Draft.

The same draft year saw players like Auston Matthews, Patrik Laine, Clayton Keller, & Matthew Tkachuk, enter the NHL.

Formerly, the caption of the OHL’s Sarnia Sting, Kyrou made his NHL debut on October 4 in the 2018-19 season. His first goal came on December 9, 2018, in a loss to the Vancouver Canucks.

He represents Canada in international games & won a gold medal in the 2018 World Junior Ice Hockey Championship.

Kyrou is on an eight-year contract worth $65 million with the Blues; he signed the deal on September 12, 2022.

Jordan Kyrou Sister: Who Is Matina Kyrou? Age And Wiki

The NHL player’s sister, Matina, is the middle of three children. Born in Canada on July 11, 2001, she is 22 years old as of 2024.

Matina, a brown-eyed brunette, has an age gap of three years with her elder brother, Jordan, born on May 5, 1998. As of now, the St. Louis Blues right-winger is 25.

Her younger brother (youngest of three), Christian, was born on September 16, 2003, which makes him 20 now.

Unlike her NHL star brother, she doesn’t have a Wikipedia page. Instead, she is popular as an Instagram model and TikTok star.

As of this writing, her Instagram handle (@matinakyrou) has more than 15.3K followers while her TikTok followers (@kyroubabe) count more than 97K.

On her social media, she has collaborations with brands like Bella Barnett and PQ Swim. She is a fan of her brother and often her posts highlight her NHL star brothers’ games.

Matina graduated from Medway High School in 2019, located in Arva, Ontario. In high school, she was a track and field athlete, however, there is no information about her college or if she even enrolled.

Jordan Kyrou Family Background: Parents And Ethnicity

Jordan is the eldest of three kids of his parents, Aki and Roula Kyrou. His father & mother met while attending the University of Toronto.

Since Kyrou’s dad, Aki, was a hockey enthusiast who even played for the Toronto Red Wings, it was evident that Jordan would pick the same. The NHL player’s mom, Roula Economou Kyrou, is a retired nurse.

As mentioned above, the ice hockey player has two siblings (both younger), a sister Matina, and a brother, Christian.

Christian followed his footsteps into hockey and was the 50th overall pick of the Dallas Stars in the 2022 NHL Entry Draft.

The 5 feet 11 inches tall player currently plays in the Dallas Stars’ affiliate AHL team Texas Stars.

A Canadian by nationality, the St. Louis Blues star has Greek roots. According to an article from The Athletic, his paternal grandfather, Iordanis “Jordan”, whom he is named after, was an immigrant from Greece in the 1960s.

Then-18-year-old, Iordanis met Maria Brouma (Jordan’s grandmother) at an English class when the latter was 14. Following three years, the two crossed paths again & married.

His grandpa, Iordanis, died at 66, on February 26, 2010, at the Trillium Health Centre – Mississauga.
