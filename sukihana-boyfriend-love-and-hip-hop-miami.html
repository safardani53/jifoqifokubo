Sukihana From 'Love &amp; Hip Hop: Miami' Has Called It Quits With Her Boyfriend, Kill Bill

Sukihana, the social media celeb and rapper who stars in VH1's 'Love & Hip: Miami,' has cut ties with her boyfriend, "Kill Bill." Details inside.

Rapper and social media star Sukihana — featured on VH1's Love & Hip Hop: Miami — is a sex-positive force to be reckoned with. The 29-year-old gained a huge following after going viral for her Kodak Black remix, "Kodak Snack" in 2017. She now has 1.5 million followers on Instagram and has released her new single, "Everywhere," on all music platforms.

On Sukihana's Instagram, the rapper is usually shown embracing her sexuality and loving on her boyfriend, Kill Bill: The Rapper. But now, it turns out that Sukihana has officially called it quits on their relationship. Here's what we know.

Sukihana has officially ended her relationship with her boyfriend, Bill.

On Sept. 21, 2021, Sukihana took to social media to share that she had decided to cut ties with her boyfriend, Kill Bill. In the now-deleted post, the rapper explained that her "mother, producers, and friends have stepped in and are forcing her to leave" the relationship.

In the deleted post, which was captured by The Shade Room, Sukihana shared a text message conversation between her and her mother — who is also her manager — that shows her mom explaining how Sukihana's relationship with Kill Bill is hurting her music career.

Although Sukihana is hurt by having to let Kill Bill go, she explained that she loves him and wishes him all the best. The breakup comes after Bill's Instagram Live session showed the couple having a verbal spat.

In Kill Bill's clip, which was captured by The Savage Room, Sukihana can be heard repeatedly asking Bill to leave their home, and him refusing, claiming that it's his house.

In another video clip, also captured by The Savage Room, Kill Bill explained his side of the breakup, saying that one thing he won't allow anyone to do is make him feel like a bad person. Kill Bill also claimed that he was "always supportive" of Sukihana and that he can't keep fighting for the relationship if the rapper chooses not to.

Kill Bill and Sukihana first became Instagram-official in Dec. 2019.

Even though Sukihana and Kill Bill are officially over, their relationship had a long run. Kill Bill first posted Sukihana on his Instagram page in Dec. 2019, though the post has since been deleted. The video of the Love & Hip Hop star was captioned, "Keeping my baby on point at her videoshoot."

Who has Sukihana dated in the past?

We know that Suki was previously dating rapper and Love & Hip Hop star Khaotic. The two allegedly broke up after Khaotic filmed Sukihana without her consent.

It's sad to see two people in love go their separate ways, but we wish Sukihana and Kill Bill all the best. After all, there seems to be a possibility that the pair could reconcile in the future.
