Brad Pitt, 53, lost his good friend Chris Cornell, 52, when he died on May 18, and he was absolutely heartbroken. “He’s also very concerned about Chris’ 3 kids,” says the source. Chris left behind three beautiful children: Toni Cornell, 12, Lillian Cornell, 17, and Christopher Cornell, 11. “It breaks his heart that they have to see those pics.” Brad is going to make sure that the three of them, and their mother Vicky Karayiannis, are taken care of. “He’s such a good friend and always reaches out to those kids to let them know he’s there for them if they need anything at all.”
