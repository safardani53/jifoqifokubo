Alex Gaskarth Biography

While Alex was in his high school, he met love of his life Lisa Ruocco. They became close friends from that time on. He proposed her in 2015 and they got married on 9th of April 2016. They also had on and off relationship when they were in high school. His wedding was held in United States. His nationality American and British and he is of white ethnicity. He is now aged 28 years old and his height is six feet tall. He also loves to spend his days with his parents.

Though Alex was born in Essex, England, he has been living in Baltimore, Maryland since he was six. He also attended some elementary school there where he met his close friend Jack Barakat. As both of them were interested in music, they expressed their desire to form a musical band. They joined with Zack Merrick and Rian Dawson and together they formed an American pop punk band All Time Low in 2003. They signed a recording deal with Emerald Moon in 2004 and their debut album The Party Scene was finally released on 19th of July 2005. Their second album So Wrong, It's Right was released on 25th of September 2007.
